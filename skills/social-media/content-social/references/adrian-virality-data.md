## 7-Day Story Sequence (Detailed Playbook)

### Day 1: Pain Point Callout
**Goal:** Get the viewer nodding. They should think "that's me."
**Formula:** [Current painful situation] + [consequence they feel daily] + [subtle hint there's a better way]
**Example:** "Still manually responding to Instagram DMs at 2am? Your VA probably sleeping while leads ghost. There's a reason your response time averages 4 hours..."
**Platform note:** Instagram Stories: use poll sticker ("Are you doing this manually? Yes/No") to boost engagement signal.

### Day 2: Case Study Proof
**Goal:** Show transformation is real and measurable.
**Formula:** [Specific client/brand] + [before metrics] + [what changed] + [after metrics] + [timeframe]
**Example:** "This ecom brand went from 12% booking rate with VAs to 34% with our AI setter in 3 weeks. Here's what their dashboard looks like now."
**Key:** Use screenshots or screen recordings of actual metrics. Blurred names are fine. The numbers are what sell.

### Day 3: Objection Handle
**Goal:** Kill the #1 reason people don't buy.
**Formula:** [State the objection honestly] + [counter with proof] + [show don't tell]
**Example:** "Does it sound like AI? I'll show you a real conversation. You tell me which one is the human."
**Key:** Pick ONE objection per day. Don't try to handle 5 at once. The most common objections: "Does it work for my niche?", "Will people know it's AI?", "What if leads want a human?"

### Day 4: Urgency / Speed Proof
**Goal:** Show the speed advantage that makes the old way feel painful.
**Formula:** [Result that happened automatically] + [while you were doing something fun] + [your old way couldn't do this]
**Example:** "Just had 3 calls book while I was sleeping. Woke up to $4,200 in new pipeline. Your VA can't do that at 3am."
**Key:** The contrast between "sleeping" and "results happening" is the emotional hook.

### Day 5: Social Proof Stack
**Goal:** Overwhelm with evidence. Make skepticism feel irrational.
**Formula:** [Big aggregate number] + [from specific channel] + [in specific timeframe]
**Example:** "247 calls booked last month from Instagram DMs alone. Zero human involvement. Here's the actual calendar."
**Key:** Stack multiple proof points if possible: testimonials, screenshots, revenue numbers.

### Day 6: Direct Offer
**Goal:** Convert the warmed-up audience.
**Formula:** [Simple CTA] + [keyword trigger] + [what they get]
**Example:** "DM me 'AI' and I'll show you exactly how it works for your business. Takes 15 minutes."
**Key:** Comment-gated CTAs ("DM me [keyword]") outperform link-in-bio by 3-5x because they start a conversation.

### Day 7: Scarcity Close
**Goal:** Create FOMO for fence-sitters.
**Formula:** [Limited availability] + [reason for limit] + [deadline]
**Example:** "Only taking 5 new clients this week then closing doors. Onboarding takes hands-on time and I refuse to half-ass it."
**Key:** The scarcity reason must feel genuine. "Only 5 because onboarding takes time" > "Only 5 because reasons."

## Reels Scripting Templates

### Template 1: Specific Number Hook
"I [verb] [specific large number] [things] and here's the only [thing] that works"
Example: "I've sent 847,000 Instagram DMs and here's the only opener that works"

### Template 2: Controversy / Hot Take
"Everyone says [common belief]. They're [wrong/poor/lying]. Here's why."
Example: "Everyone says Instagram is dead. They're poor. Here's why."

### Template 3: Before/After Transformation
"How I went from [bad metric] to [good metric] in [short timeframe] with one change"
Example: "How I went from $8k/mo to $50k/mo in 6 weeks with one change"

### Template 4: "Everyone's Wrong"
"Here's why everyone's wrong about [topic everyone has an opinion on]"
Example: "Here's why everyone's wrong about using VAs for DM management"

## Content Type Performance Breakdown

| Type | Avg Performance | Best For |
|------|----------------|----------|
| Controversy / hot take | Highest reach, polarizing | Awareness, algorithm boost |
| Before/after proof | High reach, high trust | Conversion, credibility |
| Case study narrative | Medium reach, very high trust | Bottom-of-funnel, sales |
| Tool demo / screen recording | Medium reach, high conversion | Product-focused, DM triggers |
| Personal failure story | Medium reach, high engagement | Relatability, community |
| Generic tips / how-to | Low reach (AI era problem) | SEO only, not social |
| Motivational / inspirational | Lowest reach | Avoid entirely |

## Key Insight

In the AI era, educational "how-to" content is dying because AI answers those questions better and faster. What still works: personal stories that only you can tell, specific proof that only you have, contrarian takes that break the consensus, and tool demos that show (not tell) the result.
