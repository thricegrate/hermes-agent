# Creator Organic Social Hooks

~300 fill-in-the-blank hooks for personal-brand creators building in public on TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and Instagram/LinkedIn. Different flavor from `app-marketing-hooks.md` (which is app/SaaS marketing) and `static-headline-formulas.md` (which is static ad headlines): these are for the creator voice — niche positioning, product recommendations, pain-point callouts, personal stories, hot takes.

Use when the founder, creator, or team member is the on-camera voice. Works for build-in-public the product content, skill demo stories, and founder lifestyle posts.

<a name="how-to-use"></a>
## How to use

- Swap every `(bracketed variable)` or `___` with a concrete, specific detail. Vague kills.
- Test 3–5 variants per post. Double down on winners.
- Pair with the 5 insights in `app-marketing-hooks.md` Section 3 (specificity, emotional delivery, contradiction, interactivity, achievability).
- Kill em-dashes per project rules. Use ellipses or commas.
- Coffee-shop rule: if it sounds like a LinkedIn guru, rewrite it.

## Authority & Niche Positioning

Open with who you are. Pre-qualifies the audience.

1. I'm a (niche) major and this is the most valuable tip anyone has given me
2. I'm a (professional title) in (authority qualifier) and this is how I (action)
3. As a (job title) at (location), here's a day in my life
4. I'm a (professional / authority) and I've tried (number) different (tools / ideas) so you don't have to

7. How much money do I make as a (business title / niche / professional)? Let's talk because salary transparency matters.
8. My name is (name), I work as (job title) in (industry), I live in (location)

## Usage note

- Pick 3–5 per week and stress-test them for one content cycle.
- Track which hook categories drive replies, shares, and saves for this specific audience. They'll cluster — probably 2–3 categories carry all the weight.
- When one hits, don't move on. Run 5 more variations of that hook pattern before switching.
- Pair with the 5 insights in `app-marketing-hooks.md` Section 3 for best results.
