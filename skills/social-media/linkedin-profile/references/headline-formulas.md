# LinkedIn Headline Formulas

50+ formulas for coaches, consultants, and B2B service providers. Every headline must answer: "Who do you help and what result do you get them?" in under 120 characters.

## The Rules

- Written for the CLIENT, not for you
- Specific > clever. Clarity beats creativity every time.
- A stranger should know what you do in 3 seconds
- No buzzword soup ("Visionary | Thought Leader | Changemaker")
- Max 1 emoji if any. Zero is fine.
- Test: read it to someone who doesn't know you. Can they explain what you do?

## Formula Categories

### Category 1: I Help [WHO] [OUTCOME]

The workhorse. Simple, clear, impossible to misunderstand.

1. `I help [WHO] [achieve OUTCOME]`
2. `I help [WHO] [achieve OUTCOME] without [PAIN]`
3. `I help [WHO] [achieve OUTCOME] in [TIMEFRAME]`
4. `I help [WHO] go from [BEFORE] to [AFTER]`
5. `I help [WHO] [OUTCOME] using [METHOD]`

**Examples:**
- "I help B2B coaches book 10+ sales calls/week without cold DMs"
- "I help SaaS founders go from $0 to $10K MRR in 90 days"
- "I help consultants land $5K+ clients using LinkedIn content"

### Category 2: [ROLE] | [RESULT] for [AUDIENCE]

Establishes authority while keeping it client-focused.

6. `[Role] | Helping [WHO] [OUTCOME]`
7. `[Role] | [SPECIFIC RESULT] for [WHO]`
8. `[Role] for [WHO] who want [OUTCOME]`
9. `[Expertise] Expert | [RESULT] for [WHO]`
10. `[Role] | Turning [INPUT] into [OUTPUT] for [WHO]`

**Examples:**
- "Sales Coach | Helping consultants close $10K+ deals with confidence"
- "LinkedIn Strategist | 3x more inbound leads for B2B founders"
- "Revenue Coach for agency owners who want to break $50K/mo"

### Category 3: [OUTCOME] for [WHO] | [PROOF]

Leads with the result, backs it with credibility.

11. `[OUTCOME] for [WHO] | [PROOF POINT]`
12. `[OUTCOME] for [WHO] | [NUMBER] clients served`
13. `[OUTCOME] for [WHO] | Featured in [PUBLICATION]`
14. `[OUTCOME] for [WHO] | [FRAMEWORK NAME]`
15. `[OUTCOME] for [WHO] | Ask me how`

**Examples:**
- "6-figure launches for course creators | 200+ clients served"
- "$50K+ months for coaches | The 4-Step Close System"
- "Predictable leads for consultants | 12 years in B2B sales"

### Category 4: [QUESTION/HOOK] | [WHAT YOU DO]

Pattern interrupt. Stops the scroll.

16. `Struggling to [PAIN]? I fix that for [WHO]`
17. `What if [OUTCOME] was predictable? | [WHAT YOU DO]`
18. `Your [THING] is broken. I fix it. | [ROLE] for [WHO]`
19. `[BOLD CLAIM] | Here's how I do it for [WHO]`
20. `[NUMBER] [WHO] can't be wrong | [WHAT YOU DO]`

**Examples:**
- "Struggling to fill your calendar? I fix that for B2B coaches"
- "What if getting clients was predictable? | Lead gen for consultants"
- "200+ coaches scaled past $20K/mo | I'll show you how"

### Category 5: [METHOD/FRAMEWORK] Creator | [WHO] I Serve

For those with a named methodology.

21. `Creator of [FRAMEWORK] | Helping [WHO] [OUTCOME]`
22. `The [METHOD] Guy/Gal | [OUTCOME] for [WHO]`
23. `[FRAMEWORK] | The system [WHO] use to [OUTCOME]`
24. `Built [FRAMEWORK] | Now teaching [WHO] to [OUTCOME]`
25. `Inventor of [METHOD] | [WHO] use it to [OUTCOME]`

**Examples:**
- "Creator of The Revenue Sprint | Helping coaches hit $30K months"
- "The LinkedIn Funnel Guy | Inbound clients for B2B service providers"
- "Built the 90-Day Scale System | Now teaching agency owners to use it"

## Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)

| Bad Pattern | Why It Fails | Fix |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| "Entrepreneur \| Visionary \| Thought Leader" | Says nothing about who you help or what you do | Pick ONE role + ONE audience + ONE outcome |
| "CEO at [Company Name]" | Only works if the company name means something to your audience | Add what the company does for whom |
| "Passionate about helping people succeed" | Generic, applies to everyone | Replace with specific WHO + specific OUTCOME |
| "Award-winning professional with 20 years experience" | Credentials without context. 20 years doing what for whom? | Lead with client outcome, add proof as secondary |
| Emoji overload: "🚀💰🔥 Growth Hacker \| Speaker \| Author" | Looks spammy, reduces credibility for B2B | Max 1 emoji, only if it adds clarity |
| "Open to opportunities" | Signals you're job-seeking, not client-seeking | Remove entirely, replace with client-facing headline |
| "Jack of all trades" | If you help everyone, you help no one | Niche down: pick ONE ideal client type |
