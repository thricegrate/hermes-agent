# LinkedIn Comment-Gated Growth Loop (Velocity Framework)

**Source:** Daniel Bustamante (Velocity) -- 9,000+ email subscribers from LinkedIn alone, 15K+ followers, 1M+ impressions in 10 months.

## The Growth Loop

1. Create a valuable lead magnet (use naming formula: [Desired Outcome] + [Topic] + [Container Word])
2. Write a 6-part viral giveaway post (see below)
3. Require comment to access ("Comment '10k' and I'll DM you")
4. Every comment surfaces post to commenter's network (algorithmic boost)
5. DM landing page link after comment
6. Landing page captures email
7. Confirmation page upsells or qualifies (application model)
8. Repeat with new lead magnet every 2-4 weeks

**Results from 24 launches in 10 months:**
- 1,000,000+ LinkedIn impressions
- ~15,000 LinkedIn followers
- 9,000 email subscribers
- Best post: 3,704 comments, 1,464 likes
- Single post: 700 email subs, 83%+ opt-in rate
- 90-95% of conversions happen in first 48 hours

## The 6-Part Post Framework

1. **Hook** -- curiosity gap + specific numbers in first 2-3 lines. End with colon for "see more" click.
2. **Big Promise** -- state transformation in 3 seconds after expand
3. **Reason Why** -- explain why giving it free (builds trust + compliance)
4. **Offer Stack** -- bullet list of all deliverables with price anchoring ("2 templates + 2 AI prompts + 1 guide")
5. **Crystal Clear CTA** -- "Like + comment [keyword] and I'll DM you"
6. **Strategic PS** -- objection handling, social proof, or bonus tease

## Why Comment-Gating Works for Newsletter Growth

- LinkedIn algorithm weights comments > likes > reactions
- Each comment = free distribution to commenter's entire network
- Keyword CTA is low friction (type one word)
- DM delivery creates 1:1 connection (relationship, not just subscriber)
- Connection requirement grows your LinkedIn network simultaneously
- Post stays alive longer due to ongoing comment activity

## The Series Format for Sustained Growth

"30 Days of [Topic]" series format:
- Creates posting constraint (no decision fatigue)
- Each day = new engagement spike
- Entire series repurposes into newsletter content + lead magnets + paid products
- Example: "30 Days of AI Prompts" built ongoing content library

## Pre-Validation: Only Build What Already Works

Don't guess which lead magnets will grow your list:
1. Use Taplio to sort your LinkedIn posts by engagement (30-90 day window)
2. Pick posts that are tactical, actionable, and align with content pillars
3. Expand those posts into lead magnets (add templates, prompts, checklists)
4. "Value = usefulness." Only expand what genuinely helps implementation.

## Lead Magnet Packaging (Critical for Conversion)

Two mistakes kill conversion:
1. Generic names ("Free PDF Guide") -- use naming formula instead
2. Ugly visuals -- use professional book-style or software box mockups

Naming formula: [Desired Outcome] + [Topic] + [Container Word]
Container words: Kit, Prompt, Blueprint, Template, Playbook, Roadmap, Swipe File, Launchpad
