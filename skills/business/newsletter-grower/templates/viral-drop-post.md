# Viral Drop Post Templates

Ready-to-use social media post templates for launching a viral drop campaign. Customize the bracketed placeholders with your specific details.

## X/Twitter Thread Template

```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
I just spent [time] creating [resource name].

It's [what it is] that will help you [outcome].

Giving it away free. Here's how to get it 🧵

Tweet 2-5 (Value Preview):
Here's what's inside:

[Emoji] [Section/Chapter 1]: [Brief description]
[Emoji] [Section/Chapter 2]: [Brief description]
[Emoji] [Section/Chapter 3]: [Brief description]
[Emoji] [Section/Chapter 4]: [Brief description]

Tweet 6 (Social Proof, if applicable):
[X] people have already downloaded this and here's what they're saying:

"[Quote 1]"
"[Quote 2]"

Tweet 7 (CTA):
Want it? Simple:

1. Follow me @[handle]
2. Like + Repost this thread
3. Reply "[keyword]"

I'll DM it to you.

(Must be following so I can DM)
```
