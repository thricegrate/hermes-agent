# PS Templates (8 Proven Patterns)

The PS (postscript) is the most-read section of any email after the subject line. Readers who scroll to the bottom are your most engaged subscribers. Use a PS in every single newsletter issue, rotating through these 8 proven patterns.

## How to Use

Replace the bracketed placeholders with your specific details. Rotate through the patterns so your audience doesn't see the same format twice in a row. Match the PS pattern to the issue's content and your current business goals.

---

### PS #1: Direct CTA
PS... If you want to [achieve specific outcome], check out [Product Name]. It's a step-by-step [type] that helps you [primary benefit].
[Link]

---

### PS #2: Social Proof
PS... Join [X]+ [people/creators/professionals] who are already [achieving outcome] with [Product Name].
[Link]

---

### PS #3: Refund/Guarantee
PS... Still on the fence? We have a 100% refund policy if you [condition]. And if you [alternative condition], we'll [accommodation]. So what are you waiting for?
[Link]

---

### PS #4: Testimonial
PS... Here's what [Name] had to say after [using product/joining newsletter]:
"[Testimonial quote]"
[Link to get the same result]

---

### PS #5: Scarcity/Urgency
PS... This [offer/bonus/price] is only available until [date/time]. After that, [consequence: price goes up / bonus disappears / doors close].
[Link]

---

### PS #6: Bonus Stack
PS... When you join [Product Name] today, you also get:
- [Bonus 1] ($X value)
- [Bonus 2] ($X value)
- [Bonus 3] ($X value)
That's $[total] in bonuses, free.
[Link]

---

### PS #7: Question/Engagement
PS... I'm curious, what's your biggest challenge with [niche topic] right now? Hit reply and let me know. I read every response.

---

### PS #8: Cross-Promotion / Referral
PS... If you're enjoying [Newsletter Name], please consider forwarding this to a friend who'd benefit. [Referral reward: When you refer 3 friends, you'll unlock [bonus content].]
