# Bustamante 6-Figure Launch Playbook: Strategy Reference

Source: Daniel Bustamante (Velocity), used for Dickie Bush & Nicolas Cole's Substack Starter Sprint. Strategy-level insights for launch planning. For full email copy and templates, see `newsletter-automator/references/bustamante-launch-playbook.md`.

## Launch Benchmark Data

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Units sold | 302 at $800 |
| 2-pay rate | 14% (rest paid in full) |
| VIP upsells | 12 at $2,500 = $30,000 |
| Community trials | 138 at $99/mo (60% of eligible) |
| Cash collected | $185,500 |
| Total revenue | $195,900 |
| Outstanding | $10,400 (from 2-pay) |
| Primary channel | Email (vast majority of revenue) |
| Secondary | Retargeting ads + social posts |

### Key Conversion Math
- ~40K email list -> 302 units = ~0.75% list-to-buyer conversion at $800
- 302 buyers -> 12 VIP upgrades = 4% upsell conversion
- ~230 eligible -> 138 trials = 60% free trial uptake
- Revenue per subscriber on list: ~$4.90 ($195.9K / 40K)

## Email as Primary Channel: Validation

This launch validates the ORB framework's emphasis on owned channels. Over 90% of revenue came from email. Retargeting ads and social posts were supplementary (awareness + retargeting, not primary conversion).

**Implication for launch planning:** For creators with established email lists (10K+), email should be the primary launch channel. Paid ads are for retargeting non-converters, not cold acquisition during launch week.

## The Full Launch Pipeline

```
[1] Presell / Waitlist Build (presell-validator)
    48-hour demand test. 3 assets: email, social, landing page.
    Result: validated demand + warm waitlist.
         ↓
[2] Pre-Launch: Sell the POV (newsletter-automator)
    5-7 emails over 1-2 weeks. Methodology-first, not value-drip.
    Educate on the philosophy so buying feels logical.
    Waitlist CTA with live counter in every email.
         ↓
[3] Launch Week: 4-Part System (newsletter-automator)
    Running IN PARALLEL during the 7-day launch window:
    - Part A: 10 launch emails to full list (Day 1 double, Day 7 triple)
    - Part B: 4 objection PM emails to sales page clickers only
    - Part C: 3 abandoned cart emails (self-segmentation survey)
    - Part D: LTV bolt-ons (VIP tier, community trial, 2-pay)
         ↓
[4] Post-Launch (newsletter-automator + email-sequence)
    Onboarding sequence for buyers.
    High-ticket nurture for non-buyers.
    Community onboarding for trial users.
```

## Cadence Architecture

The Bustamante launch uses variable cadence, not flat 1-per-day:

| Day | AM Email (Full List) | PM Email (Clickers Only) |
|-----|---------------------|-------------------------|
| Day 1 | Announcement + Bump | -- |
| Day 2 | Success Story | Price objection |
| Day 3 | Forward-Leaning | "Will it work for me?" |
| Day 4 | Bonuses Overview | Comparison objection |
| Day 5 | Testimonial Roundup | Offer-specific objection |
| Day 6 | FAQ | -- |
| Day 7 | Last Chance x3 | -- |

**Day 1** gets 2 emails because the announcement is the most important email and not everyone opens Email #1.
**Day 7** gets 3 emails (morning, midday, evening) because the final day is consistently the highest-converting day.

Total: 10 launch emails + 4 objection emails = 14 emails during the 7-day window.

## LTV Maximization During Launch

Three bolt-ons that increased revenue beyond the base $800 offer:

### 1. VIP Tier ($2,500)
- 3.1x the base price
- More exclusive/hands-on version with closer team access
- 4% of buyers upgraded = $30,000 additional revenue
- Presented on the sales page and in the announcement email

### 2. Community Free Trial ($99/mo)
- Free trial for the duration of the bootcamp (+ some buffer)
- 60% of eligible buyers opted in (138 trials)
- Creates recurring revenue pipeline after the launch
- If 30% convert to paid after trial: ~$4,100/mo MRR

### 3. Two-Pay Option
- Split $800 into 2 payments
- Only 14% chose it (most paid in full)
- Reduces barrier without significantly impacting cash collection
- Outstanding: $10,400 from installments

**Lesson:** These three bolt-ons added $40,000+ to the launch ($30K VIP + $10K+ potential MRR) with zero additional marketing effort. They were simply presented as options alongside the core offer.

## Integration with Five-Phase Launch

The Bustamante playbook maps to the Five-Phase approach:

| Five-Phase | Bustamante Equivalent |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Phase 1: Internal | Presell / waitlist build |
| Phase 2: Alpha | Pre-launch methodology emails |
| Phase 3: Beta | (Skipped -- straight to launch for digital products) |
| Phase 4: Early Access | Day 1-2 of launch (waitlist gets first access) |
| Phase 5: Full Launch | Days 1-7 launch emails + objection + cart sequences |

For digital products and courses, the Beta phase is often unnecessary. The presell + pre-launch warm-up replaces it.
