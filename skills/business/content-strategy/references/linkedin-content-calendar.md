# LinkedIn Content Calendar Framework

Map your content pillars and build a full month of LinkedIn posts tied to your offer. This framework uses the Pain-Education-Proof model and outputs a ready-to-execute 4-week calendar.

## Prerequisites

- Clear ideal client definition (from `niche-finder` or `linkedin-profile` audit)
- Your offer / method / framework name
- 1-3 proof points (client results, personal results, case studies)
- Desired posting frequency (3-5x/week recommended for LinkedIn)

---

## The 3-Pillar Model

All LinkedIn content falls into three buckets. The ratio matters.

### Pillar 1: PAIN (40% of posts)

Content that names the problems your ideal client lives with every day. Makes them think "that's me." Builds trust by showing you understand their world before you try to fix it.

**What it does:** Attracts. Stops the scroll. Creates connection.

**Maps to:** `content-social` Tier 3 (Awareness Bridge) -- conversion-path content.

**How to generate ideas:**
- List 10 specific pains your ideal client deals with (not vague -- specific situations, thoughts, frustrations)
- For each pain, write a post angle that names it without solving it yet

**Example pain angles for a business coach:**
1. "You're working 60-hour weeks and still can't predict next month's revenue"
2. "You've tried posting on LinkedIn for 3 months and got 4 likes from your mom"
3. "Every time you raise your prices, your stomach drops"
4. "You know you should do sales calls but you'd rather clean your inbox"
5. "Your business grows when YOU push. When you stop, it stops."

### Pillar 2: EDUCATION (30% of posts)

Content that teaches your audience how to solve problems using YOUR method. Not generic advice anyone could Google. Your specific experience, your specific framework, your specific take.

**What it does:** Builds authority. Positions you as the expert. Differentiates you from everyone else teaching the same topic.

**Maps to:** `content-social` Tier 1 (Regular) -- daily traffic driving.

**How to generate ideas:**
- List 10 things you teach clients, tied to your offer
- For each, find YOUR angle -- what do YOU do differently? What's your contrarian take?
- Frame as "through my experience" not "here's a generic tip"

**Example education angles for a business coach:**
1. "The 3-step framework I use to help coaches book 5 calls/week from LinkedIn"
2. "Why I tell my clients to STOP posting tips (and what to do instead)"
3. "How I structure a $5K offer in 15 minutes using 4 questions"
4. "The single metric I track with every client (it's not revenue)"
5. "What I learned about pricing after losing 3 clients in one week"

### Pillar 3: PROOF (30% of posts)

Content that shows your method works -- client results, personal wins, behind-the-scenes. Removes the "but will it work for ME?" doubt.

**What it does:** Converts. Turns followers into buyers. Answers the unspoken objection.

**Maps to:** `content-social` Tier 2 (Value Bombs) -- breakthrough content.

**How to generate ideas:**
- List your top client transformations (before/after with numbers)
- List your personal "build in public" moments (wins, failures, lessons)
- Frame as stories, not brags. "Here's what happened" not "look how great I am."

**Example proof angles for a business coach:**
1. "Client went from $3K/mo to $18K/mo in 90 days. Here's exactly what we changed."
2. "I posted this exact framework last month. 47 DMs. 12 calls. 4 clients."
3. "Behind the scenes of my worst coaching call ever (and what I learned)"
4. "My client said 'I almost didn't sign up.' Here's what changed her mind."
5. "Month 6 results: 200+ coaches through the program. Here's the data."

## 30-Post Idea Generator

Take your 3 pillars. For each, generate 10 ideas across these formats:

| Format | What It Is | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Story** | Personal narrative with a lesson | Pain + Proof pillars |
| **List** | "5 things I learned about X" | Education pillar |
| **Contrarian** | "Everyone says X. Here's why that's wrong." | All pillars (pattern interrupt) |
| **How-to** | Step-by-step process | Education pillar |
| **Case study** | Client transformation narrative | Proof pillar |
| **Cheatsheet** | Framework, template, or quick-reference | Education (lead magnet bait) |

**Generate:**
- 10 Pain ideas (mix: 3 story, 3 contrarian, 2 list, 2 how-to)
- 10 Education ideas (mix: 3 how-to, 3 list, 2 contrarian, 2 cheatsheet)
- 10 Proof ideas (mix: 4 case study, 3 story, 2 contrarian, 1 list)

## 4-Week Calendar Template

Assumes 4 posts/week (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri). Adjust frequency as needed.

### Week 1: Establish the Problem

| Day | Pillar | Format | Topic |
|-----|--------|--------|-------|
| Mon | Pain | Story | [Personal story about the core problem you solve] |
| Tue | Education | How-to | [Your signature framework, step 1] |
| Thu | Pain | Contrarian | [Common advice in your niche that's wrong] |
| Fri | Proof | Case study | [Best client result with specific numbers] |

### Week 2: Build Authority

| Day | Pillar | Format | Topic |
|-----|--------|--------|-------|
| Mon | Education | List | [5 lessons from working with X clients] |
| Tue | Proof | Story | [Behind-the-scenes of your process] |
| Thu | Pain | List | [X mistakes your ideal client is making] |
| Fri | Education | Cheatsheet | **LEAD MAGNET POST** -- give away a resource, collect comments |

### Week 3: Deepen Trust

| Day | Pillar | Format | Topic |
|-----|--------|--------|-------|
| Mon | Proof | Case study | [Different client result, different problem] |
| Tue | Pain | Story | [Your own struggle with this problem, how you solved it] |
| Thu | Education | Contrarian | [What you'd do differently if starting over] |
| Fri | Proof | Story | [Build-in-public update with real numbers] |

### Week 4: Convert

| Day | Pillar | Format | Topic |
|-----|--------|--------|-------|
| Mon | Education | How-to | [The full framework, all steps] |
| Tue | Proof | Case study | [Transformation story with strongest emotional pull] |
| Thu | Pain | Contrarian | [Why the problem is getting worse, not better] |
| Fri | Proof | List | **LEAD MAGNET POST** -- second distribution of the resource |

### CTA Rotation

Don't use the same CTA every post. Rotate:
- Week 1: Engagement CTA ("Drop a [emoji] if this is you")
- Week 2: Comment-gate CTA ("Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you X")
- Week 3: DM CTA ("DM me [WORD] if you want the breakdown")
- Week 4: Link CTA ("Link in comments to [resource/booking page]")

---

## Content Batching Strategy

**Write all 4 posts in one sitting per week.** Here's the fastest workflow:

1. Pick 4 topics from your idea bank (1 per pillar balance for the week)
2. Write all hooks first (just the first 2 lines of each post)
3. Fill in bodies (use `content-social` post templates)
4. Add CTAs (follow the rotation above)
5. Run through `content-social` LinkedIn Pre-Flight checklist
6. Schedule or queue (or post live -- LinkedIn rewards real-time engagement)

**Time budget:** 60-90 minutes/week for 4 posts.
