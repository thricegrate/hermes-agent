# Brainstorm AI-Powered Lead Magnet Ideas

Unbundle your paid offer into a free lead magnet that solves a smaller precursor problem, with the twist that every idea either solves the problem USING AI or teaches your audience how to use AI to solve it faster.

---

## Why This Works

Most paid products solve a "big" problem. But before your ideal customers can tackle that big problem, they need to tackle smaller problems first. A lead magnet that solves one of those precursor problems builds trust and opens a loop that the full offer completes. Adding an AI component (prompts, custom GPTs, AI-powered tools) makes the lead magnet feel modern, faster, and more valuable, because your audience gets a shortcut, not just information.

---

## How to Use This Skill

When triggered, ask the user for:

> To brainstorm AI-powered lead magnet ideas, I need details about your paid offer:
>
> 1. **What type of offer is it?**: Coaching program, course, product, service, etc.
> 2. **Who is it designed for?**: Target audience
> 3. **What big problem does it solve / big outcome does it deliver?**
> 4. **How do you deliver the transformation?**: Brief description of features or method

---

## The 2-Step Process

### Step 1: Identify 10 Smaller Precursor Problems

Review the paid offer and identify **10 smaller problems** that:
- Come BEFORE the main transformation
- Can be solved quickly with a free resource
- Are painful or frustrating enough that people want quick relief
- Naturally lead to needing the full paid offer

**Output for each problem:**

**[Problem Name]**
Why it matters & how it connects to the paid offer: [2-3 sentences explaining the connection and why solving this problem opens the loop for the full offer]

---

**After presenting the 10 problems, ask the user:**

> Which problem (or problems) do you want to focus on? Pick 1-2 and I'll brainstorm 10 AI-powered lead magnet ideas for each.

---

### Step 2: Brainstorm 10 AI-Powered Lead Magnet Ideas

Once the user picks a problem, generate **10 lead magnet ideas** that help their audience solve that specific problem using AI.

Each idea must:
- Be specific, actionable, and quick to consume/implement
- Be framed as a complete solution to that small problem
- Be naturally aligned with the paid offer
- Have an AI component (e.g., AI prompt, prompt bank, custom GPT, AI-powered template, AI workflow, etc.)

**Output for each idea:**

**[Number]. [Title]**: Clear, benefits-driven name
- **Format:** Template, guide, checklist, mini-course, GPT prompt bank, Notion + AI workflow, etc.
- **Quick description:** [1-2 sentences on what it includes and how AI is involved]
- **AI component:** [Specifically what the AI element is: a prompt, a GPT, a workflow, etc.]

---

## Guidelines

- **Favor quick wins over comprehensive solutions**: The lead magnet should feel fast and achievable
- **Keep ideas realistic and easy to produce**: No 6-month build projects
- **Focus on building trust and opening a loop**: The lead magnet solves problem A, which makes them realize they need help with problem B (your paid offer)
- **Every idea must have a clear AI component**: This isn't a generic lead magnet brainstorm; the AI angle is what makes it different
- **Make the AI component accessible**: Assume the audience may be new to AI; the lead magnet should make AI feel easy, not intimidating

---

## Quality Criteria

Each idea must:
- **Solve a real precursor problem**: Not a made-up pain point
- **Include a specific AI component**: Not just "use AI to help" but exactly what tool, prompt, or workflow
- **Be producible in days, not weeks**: Quick to create for the user
- **Be consumable in minutes, not hours**: Quick to use for the subscriber
- **Naturally bridge to the paid offer**: Solving this problem should make them want the full solution
