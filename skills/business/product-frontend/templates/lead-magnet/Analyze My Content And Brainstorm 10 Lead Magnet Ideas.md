# Analyze My Content And Brainstorm 10 Lead Magnet Ideas

Mine your existing content to find the lead magnet ideas hiding in plain sight, based on what's already proven to resonate with your audience.

---

## Why This Works

Your best lead magnet ideas already exist, hidden in content that's already proven to work. Most creators sit with a blank page trying to "come up with" lead magnet ideas from scratch. But when you start with content that's already gotten engagement, you're building on validated demand instead of guessing. This skill analyzes your content patterns and turns them into actionable lead magnet ideas.

---

## How to Use This Skill

When triggered, ask the user for:

> To analyze your content and find lead magnet ideas, I need:
>
> 1. **Your recent content**: Share your posts, newsletters, or articles from the last 90 days (the more the better, at least 10-15 pieces)
> 2. **Engagement data** (if available): Likes, comments, shares, views, or any analytics you have
> 3. **Your niche/topic area**: What do you create content about?
> 4. **Your paid offer**: What do you sell? (so lead magnets can bridge to it)
> 5. **Your audience**: Who follows you?

If they don't have analytics, that's okay, you can analyze content quality and themes without metrics.

---

## Process

### Step 1: Content Analysis

Review all provided content and identify:

**Theme Clusters:**
- What topics come up repeatedly?
- Which themes got the most engagement?
- What questions does the audience keep asking?

**Content Types That Perform:**
- Long-form vs. short-form
- Educational vs. storytelling vs. tactical
- Lists vs. frameworks vs. case studies

**Audience Signals:**
- What do people comment about most?
- What do they save or share?
- What generates DMs or follow-up questions?

**Hidden Opportunities:**
- Topics mentioned briefly that could be expanded
- Questions asked in comments that deserve full resources
- Frameworks or processes that could become templates

### Step 2: Content Analysis Summary

Deliver a clear summary of findings before generating ideas.

### Step 3: Generate 10 Lead Magnet Ideas

Based on the patterns found, brainstorm 10 lead magnet ideas that:
- Build on proven, high-engagement topics
- Expand on content that resonated
- Address questions/gaps the audience has shown interest in
- Connect naturally to the paid offer

After all 10, highlight the top 3 recommendations with a brief explanation of why these should be built first.

---

## Quality Criteria

Each lead magnet idea must:
- **Be rooted in proven content**: Directly traceable to something that already performed
- **Not be a rehash**: It should expand, systematize, or templatize the content, not repeat it
- **Be specific enough to build**: Not vague concepts but concrete deliverables
- **Bridge to the paid offer**: Natural next step, not forced connection
- **Be feasible to create quickly**: Ideally buildable in a day or less with AI assistance
