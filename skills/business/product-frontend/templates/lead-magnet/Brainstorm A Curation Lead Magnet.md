# Brainstorm A Curation Lead Magnet

Brainstorm 10 curation-based lead magnets that organize existing tools, resources, or examples into high-value collections, so your audience gets value fast and you build trust effortlessly.

---

## Why This Works

Traditional lead magnets try to teach something new. Curation-based lead magnets organize what already exists. And people value organized collections more than you think, they don't want to spend hours researching something and will gladly trade their email for a well-curated collection. The benefits: no expert-level knowledge required, AI can accelerate the research process, they become more valuable as they grow, and they naturally position you as the implementation expert. The key is strategic curation that leads to your paid offers.

---

## How to Use This Skill

When triggered, ask the user for:

> To brainstorm curation lead magnet ideas, I need:
>
> 1. **Your paid offer**: What is it? (coaching program, course, product, service)
> 2. **Target audience**: Who is it designed for?
> 3. **Main transformation**: What big problem does it solve or outcome does it help achieve?
> 4. **How you deliver it**: Brief description of your method or features

---

## Process

### Step 1: Identify Precursor Problems

Before brainstorming lead magnets, identify the smaller problems that come BEFORE the main transformation. These are the problems people are actively trying to solve by researching, compiling, and gathering resources on their own.

### Step 2: Generate 10 Curation-Based Lead Magnet Ideas

For each idea, the curation should:
- **Solve precursor problems**: Address smaller issues that come before the main transformation
- **Save massive time**: Compile resources that would take hours/days for someone to research and gather
- **Be naturally aligned**: Lead people toward needing the paid offer as the next logical step

---

## Output Format

For each of the 10 lead magnet ideas:

**Title:** Clear, benefits-driven name that emphasizes the curation aspect

**Format:** (e.g., swipe file, database, resource directory, template collection, example library)

**What's Curated:** Specific description of what examples/resources are included

**Connection to Paid Offer:** 1-2 sentences explaining how this naturally leads to the full solution

---

## Curation Lead Magnet Examples (For Reference)

- **Email subject line swipe file** (for email marketing coaches): 500+ high-performing subject lines organized by category
- **Business idea database** (for entrepreneurship coaches): 100+ trending business opportunities with market data
- **Cold email template collection** (for sales coaches): 50+ proven scripts organized by industry/situation
- **Social media post examples** (for social media consultants): 200+ viral posts with engagement metrics

---

## Quality Criteria

Each idea must:
- **Be curation, not education**: Organizing existing resources, not teaching new concepts
- **Save real time**: Something that would genuinely take hours to compile manually
- **Connect naturally to the paid offer**: The bridge should be obvious, not forced
- **Have a clear, compelling title**: Specific enough that someone would trade their email for it
- **Be feasible to create**: Could realistically be built with AI assistance in a reasonable timeframe
