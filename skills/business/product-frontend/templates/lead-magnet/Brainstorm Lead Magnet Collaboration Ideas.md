# Brainstorm Lead Magnet Collaboration Ideas

Brainstorm collaborative lead magnet ideas that leverage two creators' combined audiences, then plan the full launch strategy so one person posts on LinkedIn while the other drives email traffic, maximizing subscribers while splitting the work.

---

## Why This Works

The viral LinkedIn giveaway strategy (create lead magnet → post offering access for a comment → DM opt-in link) already works well solo. But when two creators collaborate, the results compound: one creator publishes the post while the other sends their email list to it, creating an engagement flywheel that drives more comments, more visibility, and more subscribers than either could alone. Real result: 570+ subscribers in 72 hours, 2,300+ comments, 69,000+ views, with LESS effort than a solo launch because the work was split.

---

## How to Use This Skill

When triggered, ask the user for:

> To brainstorm collab lead magnet ideas, I need details about both creators:
>
> **About you:**
> 1. Your niche/topics you create content about
> 2. Your audience size (LinkedIn followers + email list)
> 3. Your paid offer (if any)
> 4. Your strengths (writing, design, frameworks, data, etc.)
> 5. Content/assets you already have that could be repurposed
>
> **About your potential partner:**
> 1. Their niche/topics
> 2. Their audience size (approximate)
> 3. Their paid offer (if any)
> 4. Their strengths
> 5. Where your audiences overlap

## Process

### Step 1: Identify the Overlap

Analyze where both creators' niches, audiences, and expertise intersect. The strongest collab lead magnets sit at this intersection, relevant to BOTH audiences.

### Step 2: Brainstorm 10 Collab Lead Magnet Ideas

Generate 10 ideas organized by effort level:

**Low Effort (3-4 ideas)**: Can be created in 1-2 days
- Curated collections, swipe files, checklists combining both creators' frameworks

**Medium Effort (3-4 ideas)**: 3-5 days to create
- Original guides, playbooks, or template packs that require some new content

**High Effort (2-3 ideas)**: 1-2 weeks
- Comprehensive resources, mini-courses, or tools that deliver significant standalone value

For each idea:
- **Title**: Working name for the lead magnet
- **Format**: PDF, Notion template, email course, spreadsheet, etc.
- **What each creator contributes**: How to divide the work
- **Why it works for both audiences**: The overlap angle
- **Effort level**: Low / Medium / High

### Step 3: Recommend Top 3 + Launch Gameplan

Recommend the top 3 ideas based on:
- Highest appeal to both audiences
- Best effort-to-impact ratio
- Strongest bridge to both creators' paid offers

Then provide a complete launch gameplan:

**Division of work:**
- Who creates what
- Who builds the landing page
- Who writes the LinkedIn post
- Who sends the email to their list
- Who handles DM delivery

**Launch timeline:**
- Day 1-X: Build the asset
- Day X: Partner A publishes LinkedIn post
- Day X: Partner B sends email driving traffic to the post
- Day X+1 to X+3: DM delivery to commenters
- Day X+4: Share subscriber CSV with partner

**Pro tips:**
- Have the lead magnet concept ready BEFORE pitching the partner
- Decide all logistics upfront to avoid friction during the build
- The person who posts should also handle DM delivery (or have a team member do it)

---

## Quality Criteria

Each idea must:
- **Live at the intersection** of both creators' expertise: not just one person's thing
- **Appeal to BOTH audiences**: Each creator's subscribers should find it valuable
- **Be clearly divisible**: Easy to split the work between two people
- **Work as a LinkedIn giveaway**: Compelling enough for people to comment to get access
- **Deliver standalone value**: Worth opting in for regardless of either creator's paid offer
