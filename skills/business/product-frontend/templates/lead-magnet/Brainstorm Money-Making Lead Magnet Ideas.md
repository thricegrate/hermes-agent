# Brainstorm Money-Making Lead Magnet Ideas

Create lead magnets that actually lead to sales, by first identifying the narrow precursor problem your audience needs solved right before they need your paid offer.

---

## Why This Works

Most lead magnets fail because they attract freebie-seekers instead of future buyers. The fix is strategic alignment: your lead magnet should solve a problem that comes RIGHT BEFORE the problem your paid offer solves. When someone solves Problem A (with your lead magnet), they naturally discover they need to solve Problem B (your paid offer). This 2-step process ensures every lead magnet subscriber is pre-qualified for your paid product.

---

## How to Use This Skill

This is a 2-step process. When triggered, start with Step 1:

> **Step 1:** To brainstorm lead magnets that drive sales, I first need to understand your paid offer:
>
> 1. **Your paid offer**: What is it? (coaching, course, product, service)
> 2. **Target audience**: Who is it for, specifically?
> 3. **Main transformation**: What big outcome does it deliver?
> 4. **Price point**: What does it cost?
> 5. **How you deliver it**: Brief description of the method/features
> 6. **Current pain points**: What are the top 3-5 frustrations your audience has?

---

## The 2-Step Process

### Step 1: Brainstorm Narrow Precursor Problems

Before creating any lead magnet ideas, identify 7-10 narrow problems that your audience faces BEFORE they're ready for your paid offer.

**What makes a good precursor problem:**
- It's specific and urgent (not vague or aspirational)
- It comes chronologically BEFORE the main problem your offer solves
- Solving it naturally leads to needing your paid offer
- It can be solved (or significantly helped) with a free resource

**Example:** If your paid offer is a "Complete Email Marketing System" course:
- Precursor problem: "I don't know what to write about in my newsletter" → Lead magnet: "52 Newsletter Ideas Generator"
- Precursor problem: "My subject lines get low open rates" → Lead magnet: "500 High-Converting Subject Line Swipe File"
- Precursor problem: "I don't have a lead magnet to grow my list" → Lead magnet: "Lead Magnet Brainstorming Framework"

Present the 7-10 precursor problems and ask the user to pick the 1-2 they want to focus on.

### Step 2: Generate Lead Magnet Ideas

For the chosen precursor problem(s), brainstorm 5-7 lead magnet ideas, each taking a different approach.

## Quality Criteria

Each lead magnet idea must:
- **Solve a genuine precursor problem**: Not a random freebie topic
- **Create a natural bridge**: The user should FEEL the need for the paid offer after consuming it
- **Be specific and tangible**: "Email Marketing Guide" is bad; "52-Week Newsletter Idea Generator" is good
- **Be feasible to create quickly**: Ideally buildable in a day with AI
- **Attract buyers, not freebie-seekers**: The topic itself should filter for people with buying intent
