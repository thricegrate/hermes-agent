# Generate Mockup Images For A Lead Magnet Landing Page

Create professional mockup image prompts that produce clean, conversion-boosting product visuals for your lead magnet landing pages, so your opt-in page looks polished and trustworthy.

---

## Why This Works

A professional product mockup on your landing page can significantly increase opt-in rates. When people can SEE what they're getting, even for a free digital product, it feels more tangible and valuable. But most creators either skip the mockup entirely (leaving the page feeling empty) or spend hours trying to design one in Canva. This skill generates precise image prompts optimized for AI image tools that produce clean, professional results.

---

## How to Use This Skill

When triggered, ask the user for:

> To generate your lead magnet mockup prompts, I need:
>
> 1. **Lead magnet name**: What's it called?
> 2. **Lead magnet type**: What format is it? (PDF guide, ebook, checklist, template pack, swipe file, video training, etc.)
> 3. **What's inside** (brief): Key components or sections
> 4. **Brand colors** (optional): Any specific colors you want featured?
> 5. **Style preference** (optional): Clean/minimal, bold/modern, premium/luxury, friendly/casual
> 6. **Which AI image tool?** (optional): Midjourney, DALL-E, Ideogram, or general

---

## Process

### Step 1: Determine the Best Mockup Style

Based on the lead magnet type, recommend the most appropriate mockup format:

| Lead Magnet Type | Recommended Mockup |
|---|---|
| PDF Guide / Ebook | 3D book cover with slight angle |
| Checklist / One-pager | Flat-lay document on desk |
| Template Pack | Multiple stacked documents/screens |
| Swipe File | Fanned-out collection of pages |
| Video Training | Laptop/screen showing video player |
| Toolkit / Bundle | Multiple items arranged together |
| Spreadsheet / Database | Laptop showing spreadsheet view |

### Step 2: Generate Image Prompts

Create 3 different prompt variations for the user's preferred AI image tool, each taking a different visual approach:

**Variation A: Clean Product Shot**: Isolated product on clean background
**Variation B: Lifestyle Context**: Product in an aspirational desk/workspace setting
**Variation C: Device Mockup**: Product displayed on a device (tablet, laptop, phone)

### Step 3: Provide Design Guidance

Include recommendations for:
- Optimal image dimensions for landing pages
- Text overlay suggestions (if adding title/branding in Canva after)
- Background color recommendations
- How to composite the final image

## Prompt Engineering Guidelines for Mockup Images

When writing the image generation prompts:
- Specify **"product mockup"** or **"3D render"** for clean, professional results
- Include **"white background"** or **"minimal background"** for versatility
- Add **"professional"** and **"high quality"** as quality modifiers
- Specify **"no text"** or **"minimal text"** to avoid garbled AI-generated text
- Include lighting direction: **"soft studio lighting"** works best for product shots
- For book covers: **"slight angle"**, **"3D perspective"**, **"hardcover"** or **"softcover"**
- Avoid overly complex scenes: simple compositions look more professional

---

## Quality Criteria

Each prompt must:
- **Produce a professional-looking result**: Not cartoonish or amateur
- **Be optimized for the user's AI tool**: Different tools need different prompt structures
- **Be versatile**: Work on any landing page background
- **Include enough detail**: So the user doesn't need to iterate 20 times
- **Come with post-processing guidance**: Because the mockup alone rarely goes straight to the page
