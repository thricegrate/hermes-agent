# Brainstorm A 30-Day Content Series

Create a complete 30-day content series where each day's piece builds strategically on the last, growing your audience, establishing authority, and naturally driving leads to your paid offer.

---

## Why This Works

A 30-day content series is one of the most powerful lead generation strategies because it combines consistency, momentum, and progressive value. Each day's content attracts new followers while deepening the relationship with existing ones. By day 30, your audience has received so much value that your paid offer feels like the obvious next step. This framework has been used to launch newsletters, build email lists, and generate significant revenue from content alone.

---

## How to Use This Skill

When triggered, ask the user for:

> To brainstorm your 30-day content series, I need:
>
> 1. **Your niche/topic**: What do you create content about?
> 2. **Target audience**: Who are you trying to attract?
> 3. **Your paid offer**: What product/service will this series lead to?
> 4. **Platform**: Where will you publish? (LinkedIn, email newsletter, Twitter/X, etc.)
> 5. **Content format preference**: Short posts, long-form, mixed, tutorials, etc.
> 6. **Series name/theme** (optional): Do you have a name for the challenge? (e.g., "30 Days of Email Prompts")

## Process

### Step 1: Define the Strategic Arc

A great 30-day series isn't 30 random posts. It follows a strategic progression:

**Week 1 (Days 1-7): Foundation & Hook**
- Establish the core problem/opportunity
- Build curiosity and momentum
- Easy wins that create quick engagement
- Goal: Get people following along and sharing

**Week 2 (Days 8-14): Deep Dive & Value**
- Go deeper into specific subtopics
- Provide tactical, actionable content
- Introduce frameworks and methodologies
- Goal: Establish expertise and build trust

**Week 3 (Days 15-21): Advanced & Applied**
- Advanced strategies and nuanced takes
- Case studies and real examples
- Content that separates beginners from serious practitioners
- Goal: Position your paid offer as the logical next step

**Week 4 (Days 22-28): Mastery & Bridge**
- Synthesis and integration of everything taught
- Behind-the-scenes and exclusive insights
- Content that creates FOMO for what's coming next
- Goal: Build urgency toward the paid offer

**Days 29-30: Launch & Transition**
- Recap the journey and biggest takeaways
- Transition to the paid offer or next step
- Goal: Convert engaged followers into buyers/subscribers

### Step 2: Generate the 30-Day Plan

For each day, provide:
- Day number
- Topic/title
- Brief description (2-3 sentences on what the post covers)
- Content format (tutorial, story, framework, list, case study, etc.)
- Strategic purpose (why this post exists in this sequence)

### Step 3: Add Engagement Hooks

Identify 4-6 days throughout the series that should include special engagement elements:
- Giveaway/lead magnet drops
- Interactive polls or questions
- User-generated content opportunities
- Milestone celebrations

## Quality Criteria

The 30-day plan must:
- **Follow a clear strategic arc**: Not 30 random topics but a deliberate progression
- **Build on previous days**: Later content should reference and expand on earlier content
- **Mix content formats**: Not all tutorials or all stories; variety keeps engagement high
- **Include engagement hooks**: Strategic moments designed to boost interaction
- **Bridge naturally to the paid offer**: By day 30, the offer should feel inevitable
- **Be platform-appropriate**: Content format matches where it'll be published
- **Be executable**: Each day's content should be creatable in 30-60 minutes
