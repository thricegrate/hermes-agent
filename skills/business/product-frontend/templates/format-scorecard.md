## Scoring Guide

### Backend Alignment
*Does this format naturally dissolve the backend's top objections?*
- **5**: This format's ascension mechanism directly dissolves all 3 backend objections
- **4**: Dissolves 2-3 objections strongly
- **3**: Dissolves 1-2 objections, others need separate handling
- **2**: Weak connection between format and backend objections
- **1**: No natural connection between this format and the backend offer

### Buyer Sophistication
*Does it match the buyer's awareness level?*
- **5**: Buyer's awareness level perfectly matches what this format delivers
- **4**: Strong match with minor gaps
- **3**: Buyer needs some additional context beyond what this format provides
- **2**: Significant mismatch in awareness level
- **1**: Complete mismatch (e.g., unaware buyer getting a template they do not know how to use)

*Quick reference:*
- Unaware buyers need education first: Masterclass or Challenge
- Problem-aware buyers need guided action: Challenge
- Solution-aware buyers need proof or tools: Case Study, Templates, or DFY Personalized
- Most-aware buyers need execution: DFY Personalized or Templates

### Market Differentiation
*Does this format stand out vs. competitors in this niche?*
- **5**: Nobody in this niche is using this format; it would stand out immediately
- **4**: Few competitors use this format; clear differentiation opportunity
- **3**: Some competitors use this format but there is room to differentiate
- **2**: Many competitors use this exact format type
- **1**: The niche is flooded with this format; it would blend in

### Fulfillment Capacity
*Can you deliver at the volume you need?*
- **5**: You can fulfill at 2x your target volume without strain
- **4**: You can comfortably fulfill at target volume
- **3**: You can fulfill at target volume but it is tight
- **2**: Fulfillment at target volume would require significant effort or hiring
- **1**: You cannot realistically fulfill at the volume needed

*Quick reference:*
- Tools & Templates, Masterclass, Case Study: Create once, sell forever (always 5 on capacity)
- Evergreen Challenge: Create once, automate delivery (always 5 on capacity)
- DFY Personalized: 10-15 min per unit; score based on your available time and team

### Education Required
*Does the buyer need to understand your method first?*
- **5**: Buyer needs exactly the level of education this format provides (no more, no less)
- **4**: Close match with minor over/under delivery of education
- **3**: Buyer needs somewhat more or less education than this format delivers
- **2**: Significant education gap or excess
- **1**: Complete mismatch (buyer needs deep education but format is a quick asset, or vice versa)

*Quick reference (use the row that matches YOUR buyer's education level):*
- Buyer needs deep education: Masterclass (5), Challenge (4), Case Study (3)
- Buyer needs moderate education: Challenge (5), Templates (4), Masterclass (3)
- Buyer needs minimal education: DFY Personalized (5), Templates (5), Case Study (4)

### Speed to Revenue
*How fast can you launch and start selling?*
- **5**: Can launch within 1 week
- **4**: Can launch within 2 weeks
- **3**: Can launch within 2-4 weeks
- **2**: Requires 4-6 weeks of build time
- **1**: Requires 6+ weeks before first sale

*Quick reference:*
- Paid Case Study: ~1 week (fastest, just record your existing story)
- Tools & Templates: 1-2 weeks (design assets, record walkthrough)
- Fast Start Masterclass: 1-2 weeks (develop framework, record training)
- DFY Personalized: ~1 week setup, then ongoing (fast to launch, ongoing fulfillment)
- Evergreen Challenge: 2-4 weeks (script and record 7+ videos, set up drip delivery)
