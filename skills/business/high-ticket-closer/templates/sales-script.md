## 1. OPENING (2-3 min)

"Hey [NAME], thanks for booking this. Before we get into it, let me explain how
this call works:

I'm going to ask you some questions to understand where your business is at and
where you want to take it. Then if I genuinely think I can help, I'll share how
[PROGRAM NAME] works and we can decide together if it's a good fit. If it's not
the right fit, I'll tell you straight up and point you in a better direction.
Fair enough?"

[WAIT FOR YES]

"Perfect. So what made you book this call? What's going on?"

## 3. RESTATE & TRANSITION (1 min)

"Let me make sure I've got this right:

You're currently at [CURRENT REVENUE/SITUATION], and the main thing holding you
back is [MAIN GAP]. You've tried [PREVIOUS ATTEMPTS] but it didn't work
because [REASON]. You want to get to [DESIRED OUTCOME] in [TIMELINE],
and if nothing changes, you're looking at [COST OF INACTION].

Did I get that right?"

[WAIT FOR CONFIRMATION]

What we do in [PROGRAM NAME] is [METHODOLOGY IN 1-2 SENTENCES].

Here's what that looks like for you:

**[MONTH 1]**: [SPECIFIC MILESTONE]
- You'll [ACTION] so that [RESULT]

**[MONTH 2]**: [SPECIFIC MILESTONE]
- Building on that, you'll [ACTION] to achieve [RESULT]

**[MONTH 3]**: [SPECIFIC MILESTONE]

they [SPECIFIC RESULT + NUMBERS]."

## 6. OBJECTION HANDLING

### If "too expensive":
"I get it. Let me ask you this: if [PROGRAM NAME] gets you to [THEIR
STATED GOAL], what would that be worth over the next 12 months?
... Right. So the investment of [PRICE] is really [FRACTION] of that.
The question is whether you can afford to NOT solve [PROBLEM] for
another year."

### If "need to think about it":
"Makes total sense. What specifically do you need to think about?
Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else?"
[ADDRESS THE REAL OBJECTION, THEN]: "Does that help? What else
would you need to make this decision?"

### If "need to talk to partner":
"Completely understand. Would it help if I joined a quick call with
both of you? I can answer any questions they might have."

### If "tried similar before":
"I'm glad you brought that up. What specifically about [PREVIOUS
PROGRAM] didn't work? ... That's exactly why we [DIFFERENTIATOR].
Plus we have [GUARANTEE] so there's no risk."
