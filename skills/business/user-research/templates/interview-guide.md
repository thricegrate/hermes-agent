## Interview Script (~20-30 minutes)

### Opening (1 min)

"Thanks for taking the time. I'm working on something new and want to make sure I'm building the right thing. There are no wrong answers -- I'm here to learn from your experience. Everything you share stays between us. Cool?"

[Do NOT reveal what you're building yet. Keep it vague.]

### Phase 1: Context (2-3 min)

1. "Tell me a bit about yourself -- what do you do day-to-day?"
2. "How did you first get into [topic/category]?"
3. "How much time do you spend on [relevant activity] in a typical week?"
