## TL;DR (5 bullets, read this every pre-market)

1. **AGI by ~2027 is the working timeline.** Compute scales ~0.5 OOMs/year, algorithmic efficiency adds another ~0.5 OOMs/year, "unhobbling" gains stack on top. Roughly 5 OOMs of effective compute from 2023 to 2027 puts frontier models at researcher-level capability. (Ch.1, "AGI by 2027 is strikingly plausible.") `[IN PROGRESS]`

2. **Intelligence explosion follows AGI within ~1 year.** Once models can do AI research, "100 million human-equivalents" running 24/7 compress a decade of algorithmic progress into months. (Ch.2, "Hundreds of millions of AGIs could automate AI research, compressing a decade of algorithmic progress (5+ OOMs) into ≤1 year.") `[PENDING]`

3. **Trillion-dollar physical buildout is happening NOW.** Trajectory: 100k GPUs / 10 MW / $500M in 2022 → 1M GPUs / 1 GW / $10s of billions in 2026 → 10M GPUs / 10 GW / $100s of billions in 2028 → 100M GPUs / 100 GW / $1T+ in 2030. (Ch.3 scaling table.) `[IN PROGRESS]`

4. **Power is THE binding bottleneck, not chips.** A single 2028 cluster needs power equivalent to "a small/medium US state." A 2030 cluster needs ">20% of US electricity production." Nuclear (baseload), natural gas (bridge fuel), and grid infrastructure are the rate-limiters. (Ch.3, "energy may be the biggest bottleneck to AI expansion later this decade.") `[FIRED]` for the framing; `[IN PROGRESS]` for physical buildout.

5. **AGI becomes a US government project by late 2026 / 2027.** National security framing absorbs frontier labs into Manhattan-Project-style orchestration with defense-contractor relationships. Public equity in frontier labs converts to government project execution layer. (Ch.7, "by 27/28 we'll get some form of government AGI project.") `[PENDING]`

## Appendix: Chapter-by-Chapter Synthesis

### Ch.1 — From GPT-4 to AGI: Counting the OOMs

**Thesis:** GPT-4 to AGI is roughly one more order of magnitude away. Three independent scaling vectors stack: compute (~0.5 OOMs/year), algorithmic efficiency (~0.5 OOMs/year), unhobbling (chatbot to agent). Total ~5 OOMs by 2027 = AGI.

**Key quote:** "Tracing trendlines in compute (~0.5 orders of magnitude or OOMs/year), algorithmic efficiencies (~0.5 OOMs/year), and 'unhobbling' gains (from chatbot to agent), we should expect another preschooler-to-high-schooler-sized qualitative jump by 2027."

**Trading frame:** Compute demand is structural, not cyclical. Every new frontier model requires a bigger cluster than the last. Semis (SMH), datacenter (DTCR), power (URA, GRID) are the direct beneficiaries.

### Ch.2 — From AGI to Superintelligence

**Thesis:** Once AGI exists, automated AI researchers compress a decade of progress into a year. 100M human-equivalents working 24/7. Economic growth potentially 30%/year.

**Key quote:** "Rather than a few hundred researchers and engineers at a leading AI lab, we'd have more than 100,000x that—furiously working on algorithmic breakthroughs, day and night."

**Trading frame:** Once AGI hits, compute demand does not plateau — it accelerates. Intelligence explosion creates economic value that justifies the entire trillion-dollar buildout retroactively. Defense AI gets decisive military advantage framing (Cortes/Aztec analogy in essay).

### Ch.3 — Racing to the Trillion-Dollar Cluster

**THE most trading-relevant chapter.** Physical buildout: 100k GPUs to 100M GPUs by 2030. Power demand: 10 MW to 100 GW. Capex: $500M cluster to $1T+ cluster. Energy is the bottleneck, not chips.

**Key quote:** "The most extraordinary techno-capital acceleration has been set in motion. As AI revenue grows rapidly, many trillions of dollars will go into GPU, datacenter, and power buildout before the end of the decade."

**Key quote on power:** "Right now the Marcellus/Utica shale (around Pennsylvania) alone is producing around 36 billion cubic feet a day of gas; that would be enough to generate just under 150GW continuously with generators."

**Trading frame:** Nuclear utilities (CEG, VST, TLN, OKLO) are Tier 1 multi-year holds. HBM (MU, SNDK) is a Tier 2 co-bottleneck. Natural gas (EQT, AR) is a Tier 3 bridge trade.

### Ch.4 — Lock Down the Labs

**Thesis:** Current AI labs have security level 0 of 4. China can steal model weights within 12-24 months. Need nation-state-grade security (airgapped, cleared personnel, SCIFs).

**Key quote:** "Our failure today will be irreversible soon: in the next 12-24 months, we will leak key AGI breakthroughs to the CCP."

**Trading frame:** Cybersecurity (HACK, PANW, CRWD) gets a secular tailwind. Cleared IT contractors (BAH, LDOS, SAIC) benefit from airgapped datacenter buildouts.

### Ch.5 — Superalignment

**Thesis:** Technical problem of controlling smarter-than-human AI is unsolved. RLHF breaks down above human level. Few dozen researchers globally work on it. This is where the kill switches live.

**Key quote:** "Reliably controlling AI systems much smarter than we are is an unsolved technical problem."

**Trading frame:** Mostly KILL SWITCH territory. Visible alignment incident = regulatory pause = short signal on all AI longs. Watch for major safety red-team disclosures, EU AI Act enforcement, US executive orders on frontier training caps.

### Ch.6 — The Free World Must Prevail

**Thesis:** US must beat China to AGI. Whoever gets there first commands decisive military advantage (Gulf War-scale tech edge). 2-year lead minimum needed for safety margin. Export controls "huge deal and incredibly prescient."

**Key quote:** "A 2 year vs. a 2 month lead could easily make all the difference. If we have only a 2 month lead, we have no margin at all for safety."

**Trading frame:** Defense AI (PLTR, ITA) is Tier 1. Drone warfare (AVAV, KTOS) is Tier 2. Watch China's Huawei Ascend 910B (currently 2-3x worse per dollar than Nvidia — moat is real but closing).

### Ch.7 — The Project

**Thesis:** By late 2026 / 2027, US government absorbs AGI development into Manhattan-style project. Defense contractor relationships (Boeing/Lockheed model). Trillions in congressional appropriations.

**Key quote:** "by 27/28 we'll get some form of government AGI project."

**Trading frame:** Government IT primes (PLTR, BAH, LDOS, SAIC) become execution layer. Hyperscaler equity in frontier labs (MSFT/OpenAI, GOOGL/DeepMind, AMZN/Anthropic) becomes a question mark — could be nationalized or converted to gov contractor relationship. Watch executive orders and congressional hearings as the trigger.

### Ch.8 — Parting Thoughts

**Thesis:** AGI Realism. "Basically nobody outside a few hundred people in San Francisco realizes what's about to hit us." 2030s will be utterly unrecognizable.

**Key quote:** "Before the decade is out, we will have built superintelligence."

**Notable data point:** Aschenbrenner's own Situational Awareness fund Q1 2025 holdings (per public filings cited in research): INTC 45.7%, AVGO 11.7%, ONTO 7.1%, plus positions in VST, CEG, TLN, EQT. Confirms his portfolio is concentrated semis + power. Use as cross-reference, not gospel.
