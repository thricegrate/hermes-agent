## Quality Checklist

- [ ] **CTR test:** Would I stop scrolling and click this title + thumbnail?
- [ ] **Retention test:** Does every minute earn the next? No dull moments?
- [ ] **Wow factor test:** What element makes this uniquely mine?
- [ ] **Simplicity test:** Could a teenager understand every beat?
- [ ] **Excite test:** Is the creator genuinely excited to film this?
- [ ] **Expectation test:** Does the first minute match what the title/thumbnail promise?
- [ ] **Ending test:** Is the ending abrupt? No lingering, no summary?
- [ ] **Critical components:** All verified and backed up?
