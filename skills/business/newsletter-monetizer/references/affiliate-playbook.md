# Affiliate & Beehiiv Boosts Earn Playbook

## Beehiiv Boosts Earn

### How It Works

Boosts Earn is Beehiiv's built-in referral monetization feature. You recommend other newsletters to your subscribers and earn money for each new subscriber you refer.

1. Enable Boosts in your Beehiiv dashboard (Monetization > Boosts)
2. Browse available newsletters and select ones to recommend
3. Beehiiv places recommendation widgets in your newsletter and confirmation pages
4. When a subscriber signs up for a recommended newsletter through your link, you earn $1-5 per referral

### Choosing the Right Newsletters to Recommend

Select newsletters that are:
- **Complementary, not competing**: If you write about marketing, recommend a design newsletter, not another marketing newsletter
- **Relevant to your audience**: Your readers should genuinely benefit from the recommendation
- **High quality**: Bad recommendations erode trust. Only boost newsletters you'd actually read.

### Placement Options

- **Welcome email:** Add a "Newsletters I recommend" section to your welcome sequence
- **Dedicated "Recommended Reads" section:** A recurring section in your newsletter featuring 2-3 recommendations
- **Beehiiv's built-in recommendation widget:** Appears on your subscription confirmation page automatically
- **Occasional standalone mentions:** "I've been reading [Newsletter] and it's excellent for [reason]"

### Maximizing Boosts Revenue

- Rotate recommendations periodically to keep them fresh
- Write a personal sentence about WHY you recommend each newsletter
- Place recommendations where engagement is highest (after your best content)
- Track which recommendations convert best and double down on those

## External Affiliate Programs

### AI Tools with Affiliate Programs

Many AI and SaaS tools offer affiliate commissions:

- **ChatGPT Plus / OpenAI**: Refer users to paid plans
- **Claude Pro / Anthropic**: Subscription referrals
- **Midjourney**: Image generation tool referrals
- **Jasper**: AI writing tool with established affiliate program
- **Copy.ai**: AI copywriting tool affiliate program
- **Notion AI**: Productivity tool with referral program
- **Various SaaS tools**: Check individual product affiliate pages (most B2B SaaS tools offer 20-30% recurring commissions)

### How to Integrate Affiliate Links Naturally

The key word is **naturally**. Affiliate content should never feel like an ad. It should feel like a recommendation from a trusted friend.

**Only recommend tools you actually use.** Readers can tell when you're pushing something you've never touched. Your credibility is your most valuable asset.

**Write honest reviews in your newsletter:**
- What the tool does well
- What it doesn't do well (this builds trust)
- Who it's best for (and who should skip it)
- Your personal experience using it

**Include affiliate links in "Tools I Use" sections:**
- Create a recurring or one-time "My Tech Stack" issue
- List tools by category with your affiliate links
- Update this periodically as your stack changes

**Contextual mentions:**
- When discussing a topic, naturally mention the tool you use for it
- "I write all my newsletter drafts in [Tool]: it saves me about 2 hours per issue. [affiliate link]"
- This works because it's embedded in useful content, not a standalone pitch

### Disclosure

Always disclose affiliate relationships. This is both ethical and legally required (FTC guidelines).

Standard disclosure language:
> "This is an affiliate link, I earn a commission if you sign up, at no extra cost to you."

Or shorter:
> "Affiliate link"

Place the disclosure near the link, not buried in a footer nobody reads.

## Tracking

### UTM Parameters

Use UTM parameters to track which newsletter issues drive the most affiliate conversions:

- `?utm_source=newsletter`
- `&utm_medium=email`
- `&utm_campaign=issue-[number]`
- `&utm_content=[placement-type]` (e.g., "mid-roll", "tools-section", "ps-mention")

### Affiliate Dashboards

Most affiliate programs provide their own dashboard with:
- Click tracking
- Conversion tracking
- Revenue per referral
- Payout history

Review these monthly to understand:
- Which products your audience buys most
- Which placements drive the most conversions
- Which issues generate the most affiliate revenue
- Your effective earnings per subscriber (affiliate revenue / total subscribers)

### Optimization

Track revenue per newsletter issue to identify patterns:
- Which topics correlate with higher affiliate conversions?
- Which placements (mid-roll vs. PS vs. dedicated section) convert best?
- Does mentioning a personal story increase conversions?
- What's the ideal frequency for affiliate mentions before readers tune out?

Use this data to refine your approach. Most newsletters find that 1-2 natural affiliate mentions per issue is the sweet spot, enough to generate revenue without overwhelming readers.
