## Pre-Writing Checklist

Before opening a blank document:

- [ ] Offer is defined completely (30-second explanation passes)
- [ ] Audience awareness level identified
- [ ] At least 5 voice-of-customer quotes collected
- [ ] USP and unique mechanism named
- [ ] Primary pain and primary desire identified
- [ ] Risk reversal finalized
- [ ] Top 3-5 objections listed in priority order
- [ ] Competitor angles reviewed
- [ ] Framework selected and matched to awareness level
- [ ] Big Idea passes at least 5 of 7 tests
- [ ] Buyer persona written in first person

If any of these are unchecked, research is not complete. Do not write yet.

---

## Writing Process (After Brief is Complete)

1. **Choose your framework** — do not write from scratch
2. **Write the headline options first** — write 10+ options, pick the strongest
3. **Write the opening hook** — use templates from headline-power-formulas.md
4. **Fill in the framework** — write fast, do not edit while writing
5. **Take a break** — minimum 30 minutes before editing
6. **Edit for the 4Cs** — Clear, Concise, Compelling, Credible
7. **Check objection coverage** — every objection in your brief should be addressed
8. **Run the naturalness test** — read aloud. Rewrite any sentence that sounds like a press release.
9. **Check ethics** — no fake scarcity, no fake deadlines, no manufactured urgency
10. **Proofread** — grammar tool + self-review + optional peer review
