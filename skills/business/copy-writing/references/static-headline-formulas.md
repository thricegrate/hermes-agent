## The 4 Features of Brilliant Headlines

Every high-performing static headline has all 4 of these. Miss one and the headline leaks.

### 1. Short & Scannable (3–5 words)

The brain scans a 3–5-word headline in 0.2–0.5 seconds. Longer = actual reading = slower engagement. Short headlines let the viewer process visual + text simultaneously.

Examples:
- "7-Day Weight Loss Secret"
- "Boost Sales in 30 Days"
- "5 Hacks for Better Sleep"

### 2. Power Words That Trigger Emotion

Power words trigger curiosity, excitement, trust, or urgency — the four emotions that fuel action. See the full library in [Power Words](#power-words).

Examples:
- "Shocking Marketing Mistakes" (Fear / Loss)
- "Insider Formula Exposed" (Curiosity)
- "Best-Selling Hack Revealed" (Authority / Trust)

### 3. Numbers That Capture Attention

Numbers convey clarity and credibility. They set expectations, simplify information, and boost engagement. Use numeric digits ("7"), not words ("seven") — digits are processed faster.

Examples:
- "7 Hacks for Higher ROAS"
- "3-Step Formula for Ad Success"
- "5 Creative Types That Convert"

### 4. Target Audience Callout

Addressing the audience directly pre-qualifies the viewer and boosts hook rates by up to ~35% in internal tests.

Examples:
- "Ecom Founders: Stop Ad Fatigue"
- "For Solo Entrepreneurs: Scale Faster"
- "Meta Ads Experts: Avoid This Mistake"

## Awareness-Level Targeting

The best headline depends on what the audience already knows. Map the headline to the awareness stage.

### 1. Unaware (Curiosity & Market Desire)

They don't know their problem exists. Use curiosity-first headlines that hint at a hidden truth.

- "The Secret Behind 7-Figure Brands"
- "Why Most Ads Fail... Revealed"
- "Unbelievable TikTok Ads Formula"

### 2. Problem Aware (Call Out the Problem)

They know the pain, not the solution. Name the problem directly.

- "Tired of Ad Fatigue?"
- "The Ad Problem Killing Your ROAS"
- "Ad Performance Dropping? Here's Why"

### 3. Solution Aware (Highlight USP)

They know solutions exist but haven't chosen one. Differentiate.

- "Why UGC Creators Don't Scale Ads"
- "The Creative System Built for Scale"
- "Stop Wasting Money on Iterations"

### 4. Product Aware (Social Proof, Benefits)

They know your category; they need to see why you specifically. Lead with proof.

- "Proven Creative System That 3x ROAS"
- "Join 50+ Brands Scaling to 8-Figures"
- "Why Ecom Founders Choose Us"

### 5. Most Aware (Guarantee, Discount, Urgency)

They're on the fence. Give them the nudge.

- "2 Free Podcast Ads This Month Only!"
- "Book Today — Start Scaling Next Week"
- "Limited Spots Left for Q3"

## Proven Headline Formulas

Battle-tested formulas that consistently spend on Meta and TikTok. Swap the brackets with concrete specifics.

### Formula 1: [Number] [Adjective] [Promise]

- "7 Proven Steps to ROAS Growth"
- "5 Quick Fixes for Ad Fatigue"
- "3 Hidden Levers for ROAS Gains"

### Formula 2: [Target Audience]: [Problem or Outcome]

- "Ecom Founders: Stop Wasting Ad Spend"
- "Meta Advertisers: Scale Without Limits"
- "AI Builders: Ship Apps in a Weekend"

### Formula 3: Curiosity Trigger

- "Why 90% of Ads Fail"
- "The Secret Behind Viral Ads"
- "What Top 1% Founders Do Differently"

### Formula 4: Fear & Loss

- "Stop This Mistake Killing Your Ads"
- "Warning: Ad Fatigue Is Costing You Thousands"
- "The Hidden Cost of [Common Mistake]"

### Formula 5: Authority & Proof

- "The System Behind $1M/Month Brands"
- "Proven Framework for TikTok Success"
- "How [Big Brand] Scaled to [Big Number]"

**Case reference:** A static ad using the headline "Polyglot Secrets Revealed" drove $87K purchase value out of $154K total in one month at a 42% hook rate. Structure: curiosity + authority + single noun. Transferable across niches.

<a name="power-words"></a>
## Power Words Library

Use sparingly — one power word per headline is usually enough. Two is the max.

| Emotion | Power Words |
|---------|-------------|
| **Curiosity** | Secret, Exposed, Revealed, Insider, Behind the Scenes, Hidden, Truth, What Nobody Tells You |
| **Fear / Loss** | Warning, Mistake, Never, Worst, Danger, Avoid, Killing, Broken, Failing |
| **Authority / Trust** | Proven, Best-Selling, Verified, Authentic, Certified, #1, Trusted, Official, Research-Backed |
| **Urgency** | Now, Last Chance, Closing Soon, Don't Miss Out, Limited, Today Only, Ends Tonight, Final |
| **Results** | Scale, Boost, 3x, Double, Multiply, Unlock, Transform, Accelerate |
| **Simplicity** | Simple, Easy, Fast, Quick, Instant, One-Click, Effortless, Hands-Free |

<a name="checklist"></a>
## Pre-Post Checklist

Before shipping any static headline, verify:

- [ ] **3–5 words** (max 7 if the extra words earn their keep)
- [ ] **At least one power word** mapped to the right emotion for the awareness stage
- [ ] **A number** when it makes sense (digits, not words)
- [ ] **Audience callout** when targeting is narrow enough to name them
- [ ] **Awareness match** — does the headline match what this audience already knows?
- [ ] **Specificity** — concrete noun, not generic category (Insight #1 from app-marketing-hooks.md)
- [ ] **Scan test** — can a skimmer get the promise in under half a second?

If a box is empty, rewrite. The headline is the single biggest lever — don't ship a leaky one.
