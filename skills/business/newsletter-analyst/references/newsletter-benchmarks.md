# Newsletter Industry Benchmarks

## Open Rate

| Rating | All Newsletters | Niche/AI Newsletters |
|---|---|---|
| Poor | < 20% | < 25% |
| Below Average | 20-30% | 25-35% |
| Average | 30-40% | 35-45% |
| Good | 40-50% | 45-55% |
| Great | > 50% | > 55% |

Note: Niche newsletters typically see higher open rates than broad newsletters because the audience is more targeted and engaged.
