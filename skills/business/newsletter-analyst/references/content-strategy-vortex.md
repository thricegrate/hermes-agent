# The Content Strategy Vortex

## The Core Concept

Content creates a vortex of attention. The more you post about what works, the more the algorithm rewards you, the more attention you get, the more subscribers you attract. It's a flywheel.

## Content Pillars

Identify 3-5 recurring content categories. For an AI newsletter, examples:

- AI Tools & Reviews
- AI Productivity Hacks
- AI Business Applications
- AI for Side Hustles
- AI News & Trends

## The Measurement Loop

1. Publish content across all pillars for 30 days
2. Measure: Which pillar drives the highest open rates? Click rates? Replies? Social shares?
3. Rank pillars by performance
4. Double down: Increase frequency of top 2 pillars, reduce or eliminate bottom 1-2
5. Repeat every 30 days

## Platform-Specific Performance

From CNC "Vortex" methodology:

- What works on X may not work on LinkedIn. Track performance per platform.
- The hook/idea is universal, but formatting must be native to each platform.
- Key lesson: Users engage differently on each platform. Don't copy-paste across platforms.

## The "More, Better, New" Framework

- **More**: Can you increase publishing frequency without quality drop?
- **Better**: Can you improve the quality/depth of existing content?
- **New**: Can you try a new content format or platform?

Apply ONE at a time. Measure for 30 days. Then try the next.

## Retargeting (Advanced)

Use paid retargeting to re-show your best content to people who engaged but didn't subscribe.

- **Platforms**: Meta Ads, X Ads
- **Budget**: $5-20/day is enough for small lists
- **How it works**: This turns organic attention into paid re-engagement
