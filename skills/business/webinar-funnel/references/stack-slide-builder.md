# Stack Slide Builder

Template for building the cumulative offer stack that closes the webinar.

---

## Stack Architecture Rules

1. **Each element has a standalone dollar value.** Not made up. What would someone pay for this individually?
2. **Cumulative display.** Every time you add an element, re-show all previous elements on the slide.
3. **Total value should be 3-10x the actual price.** If your price is $497, stack value should be $1,500-$5,000.
4. **Order matters.** Core product first, then bonuses from most valuable to most urgency-driven.
5. **Every bonus solves a specific objection.** "But what about X?" Each bonus answers one of those.

## Price Architecture

### Price Anchor Calculation

**What others charge for similar:** $________
**What you invested to learn/build this:** $________
**Crossed-out "expected" price:** $________ (2x your real price)

### Real Price

**Actual price:** $________
**Ratio:** Stack value is ____x the real price (target: 3-10x)

### Per-Day Justification

**Price / 365 days:** $________ per day
**Relatable comparison:** "That's less than [daily expense they already pay]"

Good comparisons by price range:
- Under $1/day: a cup of coffee
- $1-3/day: a lunch
- $3-5/day: a ride-share trip
- $5-10/day: a dinner out
- $10+/day: that subscription you forgot to cancel

### Payment Plan (If Applicable)

**One-time price:** $________
**Payment plan:** ____ payments of $________
**Total if payment plan:** $________ (should be slightly higher than one-time)

## Guarantee

### Guarantee Type

- [ ] **Unconditional (pure):** "Try it for [X] days. If you don't love it, get a full refund. No questions."
- [ ] **Conditional:** "Complete [specific actions] within [timeframe]. If you don't see [result], full refund."
- [ ] **Performance-based:** "[Specific measurable outcome] or your money back."
- [ ] **Double guarantee:** "Try it for [X] days unconditionally. Then, if you complete [actions] and don't get [result] within [longer timeframe], also full refund."

### Guarantee Details

**Duration:** ________ days
**Conditions (if conditional):** [What must they do?]
**Specific result promised (if performance):** [What outcome?]
**Guarantee statement (write it out):**
> "________________________________________"

### Guarantee Rules

- [ ] Guarantee is stated AFTER the price reveal (reduces sticker shock)
- [ ] Guarantee feels confident, not desperate
- [ ] Frame as: "I'm so confident this works that..." NOT "If you're not sure..."
- [ ] Guarantee terms are clear and specific (no ambiguity)

## Urgency Bonus

The urgency bonus is ONLY available to people who act during or immediately after the webinar.

**Bonus name:** [Name]
**What it is:** [Description]
**Why it's valuable:** [What problem does it solve that the core offer doesn't?]
**Standalone value:** $________
**Deadline:** [X hours/minutes after webinar ends]

### Urgency Rules

- [ ] The bonus must be genuinely removed after the deadline
- [ ] The deadline must be real and enforced
- [ ] The bonus should be desirable but not essential (they should want the core offer regardless)
- [ ] Announce the urgency bonus AFTER the price reveal and guarantee

## Stack Delivery Script

Use this script during the webinar close:

1. "Let me show you everything you're getting today."
2. [Introduce Element 1 + value] "If I sold this alone, it'd be worth $[value]."
3. [Add Element 2 to slide] "But you're also getting [Bonus 1]." [Describe + value]
4. [Add Element 3] "And [Bonus 2]." [Describe + value]
5. [Add Element 4] "Plus [Bonus 3]." [Describe + value]
6. [Big Stack Slide] "When you add it all up, that's $[total value] in value."
7. [If All #1] Pause.
8. [If All #2] Pause.
9. [If All #3] Pause.
10. "But it does ALL of these things."
11. [I Had Two Choices story]
12. "Others charge $[anchor]. I paid $[investment] to learn this."
13. "Your investment today: $[real price]."
14. [Per-day justification]
15. [Guarantee]
16. "And if you act in the next [X hours]: [urgency bonus]."
17. "Go to [URL] right now."

## Quality Checks

Before building slides:

- [ ] Every element has a defensible dollar value (not inflated beyond belief)
- [ ] Every bonus solves a specific, named objection
- [ ] Total stack value is 3-10x real price
- [ ] "If All" statements target different motivations (not the same one 4 times)
- [ ] Per-day cost is a number that feels small and relatable
- [ ] Guarantee removes the biggest purchasing risk
- [ ] Urgency bonus has a real, enforced deadline
- [ ] Payment plan option available if price is over $297
