# Perfect Webinar: 43-Slide Script Breakdown

Full slide-by-slide guide. Each slide includes what to show and what to say.

---

## Section 1: Intro (Slides 1-10, Minutes 0-15)

### Slide 1: Title Slide
**Show:** Webinar title + your name + date/time
**Say:** "Welcome to [title]. I'm [name], and over the next 60-90 minutes, I'm going to show you [Big Domino promise]."
**Rule:** Title is curiosity-driven. Never reveal the full content in the title.

### Slide 2: The Promise
**Show:** One sentence: "By the end of this training, you'll know how to [X], [Y], and [Z]."
**Say:** "Here's my promise. By the time we're done, you'll walk away knowing exactly how to [outcome 1], [outcome 2], and [outcome 3]. Sound fair?"
**Rule:** 3 specific outcomes. Not vague. Not "change your life." Concrete.

### Slide 3: The Hook (Blair Warren Lever 1-2)
**Show:** Empathy statement or question
**Say:** Pick 2-3 of the 5 levers:
- Encourage dreams: "You want [their dream]. That's a great goal. And it's more achievable than you think."
- Justify failures: "If you've tried [thing] and it didn't work, it's not your fault. Here's why..."
- Allay fears: "You might be worried about [fear]. I'll show you exactly why that won't be a problem."
- Confirm suspicions: "You've probably suspected that [industry BS] is true. You're right."
- Throw rocks: "The [gurus/industry] told you to [bad advice]. That's why you're stuck."

### Slide 4: The Ruler
**Show:** Horizontal line. Left end: "YOU ARE HERE" with current painful state. Right end: "YOU'LL BE HERE" with desired state. Arrow connecting them.
**Say:** "Right now, you're somewhere on this line. Maybe you're [describe pain]. By the end, I'm going to show you exactly how to get to [describe outcome]. My job is to close this gap."

### Slide 5: Big Domino Statement
**Show:** The Big Domino statement in bold text
**Say:** "Here's what I believe, and what I'm going to prove to you today: [Big Domino]. If I can show you that this is true, everything else falls into place."

### Slide 6: About Me (Qualify)
**Show:** 2-3 key credibility markers. Results, not resume.
**Say:** Keep it under 2 minutes. "[Result 1]. [Result 2]. [Result 3]. But I didn't start here. Let me tell you how I got here."
**Rule:** Show results that are relevant to THEIR goals. Nobody cares about your degree. They care about your proof.

### Slide 7: Origin Story - Setup
**Show:** Photo or visual from "before" state
**Say:** Start the Epiphany Bridge origin story. Set the scene. Where were you? What did you want? What was the problem?

### Slide 8: Origin Story - Conflict
**Show:** Visual of the struggle or obstacle
**Say:** What wall did you hit? What went wrong? What did you try that failed? Make this relatable to their current state.

### Slide 9: Origin Story - Epiphany
**Show:** The "aha moment" visual or key quote
**Say:** "And then [the epiphany]. That's when I realized [new belief]. Everything changed."

### Slide 10: Origin Story - Result + Transition
**Show:** Before/after or key result metric
**Say:** "Since that moment, [results]. And that's exactly what I'm going to break down for you in the next 3 secrets. Grab a pen. Close your other tabs. Let's go."
**Rule:** This is the "command attention" moment. Be direct. Tell them what to do.

## Section 2: Three Secrets (Slides 11-25, Minutes 15-60)

### SECRET 1: Vehicle Belief (Slides 11-15)

### Slide 11: Secret 1 Title
**Show:** "Secret #1: [Curiosity-driven title]"
**Say:** "Most people believe [false belief about the vehicle]. I used to believe it too. Let me show you what actually works."

### Slide 12: Secret 1 - Epiphany Bridge Story
**Show:** Story visual (photo, screenshot, or simple graphic)
**Say:** Tell the story that breaks the Vehicle false belief. Follow the 14-question script. 3-5 minutes. End with: "That's when I realized [new belief about the vehicle]."

### Slide 13: Secret 1 - Framework
**Show:** Visual framework (diagram, steps, or model)
**Say:** Teach the WHAT. Name the framework. Show the components. Explain why it works. Do NOT teach the full HOW. "This is the [Framework Name]. It has [X] parts. Here's why each matters..."

### Slide 14: Secret 1 - Case Study
**Show:** Results screenshot, testimonial, or data
**Say:** "[Name/person] used this exact framework and [specific result]. They started from [relatable starting point]. In [timeframe], they [outcome]."

### Slide 15: Secret 1 - Trial Close
**Show:** Question on screen
**Say:** "So let me ask you: now that you've seen how [framework] works, and you've seen [case study person] get [result], can you see how this could work for your [situation]?"
**Rule:** Pause. Let them answer (mentally or in chat). This is a commitment checkpoint.

### SECRET 2: Internal Belief (Slides 16-20)

### Slide 16: Secret 2 Title
**Show:** "Secret #2: [Curiosity-driven title]"
**Say:** "Now you might be thinking: 'That's great, but I can't do this because [internal objection].' Let me tell you a story."

### Slide 17: Secret 2 - Epiphany Bridge Story
**Show:** Story visual featuring someone who overcame the internal doubt
**Say:** Tell the story of someone (you or a client) who had the same internal belief and broke through it. "They thought they couldn't because [limitation]. Here's what actually happened..."

### Slide 18: Secret 2 - Framework (Simplified)
**Show:** The simplest version of the implementation framework
**Say:** "Here's the thing. You don't need [thing they think they need]. You need [simpler thing]. Let me show you the [X]-step version that anyone can follow."

### Slide 19: Secret 2 - Case Study
**Show:** Testimonial from someone who started with the same limitation
**Say:** "[Name] had the exact same doubt. They [specific limitation]. But they followed [framework] and got [result] in [timeframe]."

### Slide 20: Secret 2 - Trial Close
**Show:** Question on screen
**Say:** "If [person in story] could do this starting from [low point], with [same limitation you have], do you think you could too? Type 'yes' in the chat."

### SECRET 3: External Belief (Slides 21-25)

### Slide 21: Secret 3 Title
**Show:** "Secret #3: [Curiosity-driven title]"
**Say:** "The last thing holding you back: [external belief]. 'Even if this works, and even if I could do it, [external force] will stop me.' Let me show you why that's not true."

### Slide 22: Secret 3 - Epiphany Bridge Story
**Show:** Story visual or data that addresses the external objection
**Say:** Tell the story that neutralizes the external belief. Use data, proof, or narrative.

### Slide 23: Secret 3 - Framework
**Show:** Framework showing how the system handles the external obstacle
**Say:** "The [framework] actually accounts for [external obstacle]. Here's how..."

### Slide 24: Secret 3 - Case Study
**Show:** Proof from someone who succeeded DESPITE the external factor
**Say:** "[Name] was in a [saturated market / had no time / had no budget]. They still [result]. Here's exactly how."

### Slide 25: Secret 3 - Trial Close
**Show:** Question on screen
**Say:** "Now that you know [external obstacle] isn't actually a barrier, and you've seen [proof], what's stopping you from getting started?"

## Section 3: Stack & Close (Slides 26-43, Minutes 60-90)

### Slide 26: Recap
**Show:** All 3 secrets listed
**Say:** "Let's recap what you've learned. Secret 1: [summary]. Secret 2: [summary]. Secret 3: [summary]. You now have the knowledge. But knowledge without action is just entertainment."

### Slide 27: Transition Question
**Show:** Blank or simple question
**Say:** "Let me ask you something. Now that you know these 3 things, what would it be worth to have someone hand you the complete system, with every template, every script, every tool, done for you?"

### Slide 28: Introduce the Offer
**Show:** Product name + one-sentence description
**Say:** "That's why I created [Product Name]. It's [one sentence description]. Let me show you everything that's inside."

### Slide 29: Stack Element 1 - Core Product
**Show:** Core product with description + value ($X,XXX)
**Say:** Describe the core product. What it is, what it includes, what result it delivers. "If I sold this alone, it would be worth [$value]."

### Slide 30: Stack Element 2 - Bonus 1
**Show:** Core product + Bonus 1 (cumulative display)
**Say:** "But I'm also including [Bonus 1]. This solves [specific objection]. On its own, this is worth [$value]."

### Slide 31: Stack Element 3 - Bonus 2
**Show:** Core + Bonus 1 + Bonus 2 (cumulative display)
**Say:** "You're also getting [Bonus 2]. This [accelerates / simplifies / removes friction]. Value: [$value]."

### Slide 32: Stack Element 4 - Bonus 3
**Show:** Core + Bonus 1 + Bonus 2 + Bonus 3 (cumulative display)
**Say:** "And [Bonus 3]. This handles [remaining obstacle]. Worth [$value] on its own."

### Slide 33: Stack Element 5 - Bonus 4 (Optional)
**Show:** Full stack growing (cumulative display)
**Say:** Additional bonus if applicable. Same pattern.

### Slide 34: Big Stack Slide
**Show:** ALL elements listed with individual values + TOTAL VALUE in large font
**Say:** "When you add it all up, you're getting [$total value] worth of [tools/training/resources]. That's what this is worth."

### Slide 35: "If All" Anchoring #1
**Show:** "If all this did was [outcome 1], would it be worth [price]?"
**Say:** "If all this did was [specific outcome 1], would it be worth [price]? Of course it would."

### Slide 36: "If All" Anchoring #2
**Show:** "If all this did was [outcome 2], would it be worth [price]?"
**Say:** "And if all this did was [specific outcome 2], would it be worth [price]? Absolutely."

### Slide 37: "If All" Anchoring #3-4
**Show:** "If all this did was [outcome 3], would it be worth [price]?"
**Say:** "If all this did was [outcome 3], would it be worth [price]?" Pause. "But it does ALL of these things. And more."

### Slide 38: "I Had Two Choices" Story
**Show:** Simple text or photo
**Say:** "When I figured this out, I had two choices. I could keep it to myself and keep profiting alone. Or I could package everything I've built and share it with people who are where I was [X years ago]. I chose to share it because [genuine reason]."

### Slide 39: Price Anchor + Reveal
**Show:** Crossed-out higher price + real price
**Say:** "Others charge [$2x price] for less than this. I invested [$high amount] learning this myself. But you're not going to pay [$total value]. You're not even going to pay [$2x price]. Your investment today is just [$real price]."

### Slide 40: Per-Day Justification
**Show:** Price / 365 = daily cost comparison
**Say:** "That's [$daily cost] per day. Less than [relatable daily expense]. For a complete system that [primary outcome]."

### Slide 41: Guarantee
**Show:** Guarantee badge/visual + terms
**Say:** "And you're protected by my [guarantee type] guarantee. [Full guarantee terms]. You have [X days] to go through everything, implement it, and see results. If [condition], I'll refund every penny."

### Slide 42: Urgency Bonus
**Show:** Bonus available only for fast action + deadline
**Say:** "If you join in the next [X hours/minutes], you also get [urgency bonus] worth [$value]. This is only available right now. Once this timer hits zero, it's gone."

### Slide 43: Final CTA + Q&A
**Show:** URL/button + everything they get (mini stack recap)
**Say:** "Go to [URL] right now. You get [quick stack recap]. It's [price]. You're covered by [guarantee]. And if you act now, you get [urgency bonus]. Go to [URL]. I'll take questions while you're signing up."

**Q&A Rule:** Every answer to a question should end with "...and that's exactly what you get inside [Product Name]. Go to [URL]."

## Timing Cheat Sheet

| Section | Slides | Time | Goal |
|---------|--------|------|------|
| Intro | 1-10 | 0-15 min | Rapport, authority, curiosity |
| Secret 1 | 11-15 | 15-30 min | Break Vehicle belief |
| Secret 2 | 16-20 | 30-45 min | Break Internal belief |
| Secret 3 | 21-25 | 45-60 min | Break External belief |
| Stack & Close | 26-43 | 60-90 min | Present offer, close |

## Pre-Webinar Checklist

- [ ] All 43 slides built and reviewed
- [ ] Stories practiced out loud (not memorized word-for-word)
- [ ] Stack values calculated and consistent
- [ ] Payment link tested and working
- [ ] Urgency timer set and tested
- [ ] Q&A responses pre-written for top 10 expected questions
- [ ] Backup plan for tech failure (phone-in number, replay link ready)
- [ ] Water bottle, notes, and quiet room ready
