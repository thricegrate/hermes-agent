## Kill Switch Check

Check each one. Be honest.

- [ ] Email CTR on announcement < 2%? --> RED FLAG
- [ ] Landing page opt-in (warm traffic) < 20%? --> RED FLAG
- [ ] Total waitlist below threshold for your list size? --> RED FLAG
- [ ] Zero unprompted replies/DMs in 7 days? --> RED FLAG
- [ ] Unsubscribe spike > 1% on announcement? --> RED FLAG

**Kill switches triggered:** [0 / 1 / 2+]

---

## Verdict

**[ ] BUILD** -- Clear demand signal. Multiple green metrics. Unprompted interest. Proceed to `product-architect` with this data.

**[ ] PIVOT** -- Some interest but wrong angle. Signals suggest the audience cares about the *space* but not this specific *offer*. Revise the hypothesis canvas and re-test with a different angle/promise/format.

**[ ] KILL** -- Insufficient demand. Two or more kill switches triggered. Zero qualitative enthusiasm. Save the time. Move on.
