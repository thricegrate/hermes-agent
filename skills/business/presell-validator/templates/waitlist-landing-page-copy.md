# Waitlist Landing Page Copy Template

This is NOT a sales page. This is a "coming soon" page. Its only job: capture an email address from someone who's already interested enough to click.

Keep it simple. Simplicity converts.

---

## Page Structure

### Section 1: Hero

**Headline:** FREE [NICHE] [CONTENT TYPE] -- Coming Soon

**Subheadline:** [One sentence: who it's for + the outcome they'll get]

Subscribe below for early access.

---

### Section 2: The Promise (2-3 sentences max)

[TIMEFRAME], I'm launching a new **FREE [CONTENT TYPE]** to help you **[BENEFIT 1]** and **[BENEFIT 2]**.

Join the waitlist to be first in line when it drops.

---

### Section 3: Email Capture

[Email Address field]

**[Join The Waitlist]** <-- CTA button

*We respect your privacy. Unsubscribe at any time.*

---

- [What they'll learn or get]

### Who this is for (if audience is narrow)
"Built for [SPECIFIC PERSON] who [SPECIFIC SITUATION]."

---

## Template Rules

- Headline leads with FREE. That's the hook.
- Body copy is 1-2 sentences max. This page converts on simplicity, not length.
- Bold the most compelling benefit phrases.
- CTA button text is action-forward: "Join The Waitlist", "Get Early Access", "Save My Spot". Never "Submit" or "Sign Up".
- Trust line is non-negotiable. Always include privacy/unsubscribe note.
- No pricing. No modules. No bonus stacks. That's for the sales page later.
- No testimonials (you don't have them yet -- the product doesn't exist).
- Works on Beehiiv, ConvertKit, Carrd, or any landing page builder.
- Mobile-first: test how it looks on a phone before going live.
