## Pass/Fail Criteria (Define BEFORE Testing)

Set these numbers NOW. Don't change them after you see results.

**BUILD signal (need ALL of these):**
- Waitlist signups in [TIMEFRAME]: > [NUMBER]
- Email CTR on announcement: > [NUMBER]%
- Landing page opt-in rate: > [NUMBER]%
- At least [NUMBER] unprompted replies/DMs

**KILL signal (ANY of these):**
- Waitlist signups in [TIMEFRAME]: < [NUMBER]
- Email CTR on announcement: < [NUMBER]%
- Landing page opt-in rate: < [NUMBER]%
- Zero unprompted engagement in 7 days
