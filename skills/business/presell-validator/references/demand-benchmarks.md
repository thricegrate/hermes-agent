# Demand Benchmarks & Kill Switch Thresholds

Use these benchmarks to evaluate presell results in the Build-or-Kill Scorecard.

## Email Announcement Benchmarks

| Metric | Red Flag | Healthy | Strong | Exceptional |
|--------|----------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Open rate (warm list) | < 20% | 25-35% | 35-45% | 45%+ |
| Click-through rate | < 2% | 3-5% | 5-8% | 8%+ |
| Unsubscribe rate | > 1% | < 0.5% | < 0.3% | < 0.1% |

CC-specific baseline: ~36% open rate on regular issues. A presell announcement should match or beat this.

## Landing Page Opt-in Benchmarks

| Traffic Source | Red Flag | Healthy | Strong | Exceptional |
|----------------|----------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Warm list (email click-through) | < 20% | 30-50% | 50-70% | 70%+ |
| Social followers | < 10% | 15-25% | 25-40% | 40%+ |
| Cold paid traffic | < 5% | 8-15% | 15-25% | 25%+ |

The Bustamante case study hit 80%+ opt-in, but that was a warm audience from a 40K email list with strong creator-audience trust. For most creators, 30%+ from warm traffic is a solid signal.

## Waitlist Volume Benchmarks (7-day window)

Scale these to your list size:

| List Size | Kill Threshold | Healthy | Strong |
|-----------|---------------|---------|--------|
| 1K-5K | < 20 signups | 50-100 | 100+ |
| 5K-20K | < 50 signups | 100-300 | 300+ |
| 20K-50K | < 100 signups | 300-800 | 800+ |
| 50K-100K | < 200 signups | 500-1,500 | 1,500+ |
| 100K+ | < 500 signups | 1,000-3,000 | 3,000+ |

For CC (215K+): anything under 500 signups in 7 days is a red flag. Target 1,000+ for a confident BUILD signal.

## Social Post Benchmarks

| Platform | Red Flag | Healthy | Strong |
|----------|----------|---------|--------|
| X/Twitter engagement rate | < 1% | 2-4% | 4%+ |
| LinkedIn engagement rate | < 2% | 3-6% | 6%+ |
| Comment-to-click ratio | < 5% | 10-20% | 20%+ |
| DMs/replies mentioning the offer | 0 | 3-10 | 10+ |

## Qualitative Signals (Can't Be Ignored)

These don't have numbers, but they matter as much as metrics:

- **Unprompted replies**: People emailing back asking "when is this ready?" = strong signal
- **Sharing without being asked**: People forwarding the email or reposting = very strong signal
- **Specific questions**: "Will it cover X?" or "Does it work for Y?" = audience already imagining themselves using it
- **Price questions**: "How much will it cost?" = buying intent, not just curiosity
- **Zero engagement**: No replies, no questions, no DMs = the idea didn't land. Don't rationalize it.

## Kill Switch Thresholds (Summary)

These are the hard lines. If you hit any of these, the verdict is KILL or PIVOT:

| Signal | Kill Threshold | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Email CTR on announcement | < 2% | Your list doesn't care enough to click |
| Landing page opt-in (warm traffic) | < 20% | The promise isn't compelling enough |
| Total waitlist (7 days, scaled to list) | Below list-size threshold above | Not enough demand to justify building |
| Unprompted replies/DMs | Zero in 7 days | Nobody is excited about this |
| Unsubscribe spike | > 1% on announcement email | You misread your audience |

One red flag = investigate. Two or more = kill it or pivot the angle hard.
