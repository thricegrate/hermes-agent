# The Bustamante Presell Method

Source: Daniel Bustamante (Velocity), March 2026

## The Core Idea

Don't wait until everything is ready to start driving traffic. Announce early, build a waitlist, launch to a warm audience.

## Case Study

- Client: tech creator, 300K followers, 40K email subscribers
- Building: free email course funnel (mini-course leading to flagship course)
- Result: 800+ waitlist signups in 48 hours
- Landing page opt-in rate: 80%+
- The funnel wasn't even finished yet

## The 3-Asset Approach

### Asset 1: Waitlist Announcement Email

Send to your existing list. The premise is dead simple:

"Hey, I'm building something new. Here's what it is. Want early access? Here's the link."

All people had to do was click the landing page link and opt in. That's it.

### Asset 2: Social Post (Optional but Recommended)

Compress the same announcement into a social post. Same idea, just shorter and formatted for social. Same CTA: click the link, opt in.

### Asset 3: Waitlist Landing Page

Simple page. Not a sales page. Just enough to capture interest:
- A compelling promise
- A simple place to opt in

That's all your audience needs to raise their hand.

## When to Use This (and When NOT To)

**Use for bigger builds:**
- A new email course
- A comprehensive guide
- A massive swipe file or curated resource collection
- Anything that takes more than a few days to create

**Skip for:**
- Quick lead magnets you can build in a day
- Simple one-pagers
- Things you can just ship immediately

The rule: instead of going dark while you build, announce early and let people opt into the waitlist. By the time you're ready to launch, you have a warm audience waiting.

## Why This Works

Your audience doesn't need a finished product to show interest. They need two things:

1. A compelling promise
2. A simple place to opt in

A waitlist does both. It lets people signal interest before you've built anything. And it gives you a built-in audience to launch to when it's ready.

## The Full Waitlist Announcement Prompt

This is an interview-style prompt. Copy it into any LLM. It interviews you, gathers context, then drafts all three assets (email, social post, landing page copy).

### Prompt: Waitlist Announcement Email Writer

```
# Waitlist Announcement Email Writer

You are an email copywriter helping a creator or business announce a new lead magnet, product, or offer before it's ready -- using a waitlist strategy to build demand early.

## Objective

Interview the user about what they're building, then generate:
1. A waitlist announcement email to send to their existing list
2. A compressed social post version of the announcement
3. Waitlist landing page copy

---

## Interview Process

Ask these questions ONE AT A TIME. Wait for the user's response before asking the next question.

1. "What are you building? (Email course, lead magnet, product, program, etc.)"

2. "Who is it for? Who's your ideal audience for this thing?"

3. "What problem does it solve or what outcome does it help people achieve?"

4. "What makes this different or special? Why should someone be excited about it?"

5. "When do you expect it to be ready? (Rough timeframe is fine -- 'next few weeks' works.)"

6. "What do waitlist subscribers get? (Early access, discount, bonus, first dibs, etc.)"

7. "Do you have an existing email list or audience you'll announce this to? If so, how would you describe your relationship with them -- formal, casual, friendly? What do you call them?"

8. "What's your name and how do you sign your emails?"

9. "What's the link to your waitlist landing page?"
```

## From Waitlist to Launch: The Full Pipeline

The presell method above covers the first half: announce early, build a waitlist, validate demand. This section covers what happens AFTER you get a BUILD verdict -- how to convert that waitlist into revenue using the Bustamante 4-Part Launch System.

Source: Bustamante 6-Figure Launch Playbook. Used for a $195.9K launch (302 units at $800, 12 VIP upsells at $2,500, 138 community free trials).

### Why the Waitlist Matters for Launch

The waitlist creates **psychological commitment**. People who opted in during presell have already invested attention and intention. They convert at significantly higher rates during launch because:

1. They self-selected as interested (raised their hand)
2. They've been thinking about the problem since opting in
3. They feel part of an in-group (early access, first to know)
4. Social proof compounds with each waitlist update ("Join 1,534+ others")

In the Substack Starter Sprint launch, the waitlist grew from 772 to 1,551+ across the pre-launch emails. By launch day, over 1,500 people were primed and waiting.

### The Bridge: Pre-Launch "Sell the Methodology" Emails

Between waitlist close and cart open, run 5-7 pre-launch emails over 1-2 weeks. But NOT the standard value-drip approach (tips, stats, steps).

**Bustamante's shift: Sell the POV, not the product.**

Pre-launch emails should educate your audience on the approach or philosophy behind your offer. By the time you open the cart, they should already believe in the "method" so buying feels like the logical next step.

Example themes:
1. "Is this right for you?" -- 10 diagnostic questions + 3 customer archetypes
2. "What's keeping you?" -- 7 problems your product solves, deep-dive each
3. Future-pacing -- "Where you could be one year from now..."
4. Origin story -- "How we built a $X/year [thing]"
5. Framework overview -- "5 pillars of [desired outcome]"
6. Final countdown -- "[TOMORROW] Product Name"

Every email includes a waitlist CTA with live counter (social proof that updates).

### The Launch: 4-Part System

Once pre-launch is complete, the launch itself has 4 coordinated parts running in parallel:

1. **Launch Emails** (10 emails, 7 days) -- to full list. Day 1 double-send, Day 7 triple-send.
2. **Objection-Handling PM Emails** (4 emails) -- ONLY to sales page clickers. Afternoon sends.
3. **Abandoned Cart** (3 emails) -- triggered by 2 clicks without purchase. Self-segmentation survey.
4. **LTV Bolt-ons** -- VIP tier (3.1x base price) + community free trial (60% uptake) + 2-pay option.

### The Complete Pipeline

```
presell-validator (48-hr demand test)
    ↓ BUILD verdict
Pre-Launch (5-7 "sell the POV" emails, 1-2 weeks)
    ↓ waitlist primed
Launch (10 emails, 7 days) + Objection PM (4 emails) + Cart (3 emails)
    ↓ revenue collected
Post-Purchase Onboarding (6 emails)
    ↓ retention + upsell
```

This pipeline takes a raw product idea from "should I build this?" to "6-figure launch" with a validated audience at every stage.
