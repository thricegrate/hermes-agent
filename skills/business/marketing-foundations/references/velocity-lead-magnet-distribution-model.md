# Lead Magnet Distribution Model (Velocity Framework)

**Source:** Daniel Bustamante (Velocity) -- 30+ lead magnets, 1M+ LinkedIn impressions, 9K+ email subs from LinkedIn in 10 months.

## The Complete Model

### Creation (What to Build)
1. Mine top-performing posts for lead magnet ideas (pre-validation)
2. Filter: tactical + actionable + aligned with content pillars
3. Expand into deeper resource (templates, prompts, checklists)
4. Name using formula: [Desired Outcome] + [Topic] + [Container Word]
5. Professional mockup (book or box style)

### Distribution (How to Spread)
1. 6-part viral giveaway post on LinkedIn (Hook, Big Promise, Reason Why, Offer Stack, CTA, PS)
2. Comment-gated CTA ("Comment [keyword] and I'll DM you")
3. DM landing page link
4. Landing page captures email (7 rules: single CTA, outcome headline, above-fold opt-in, social proof, objection handling, mockup, naming formula)

### Conversion (How to Monetize)
1. Confirmation page delivers lead magnet + upsells
2. Application survey qualifies leads
3. "Need Help?" emails (1-2/month) convert warm leads
4. Niche Expert Breakdown newsletter format pre-sells expertise

### Flywheel
Newsletter content -> validated via engagement -> lead magnets -> new subscribers -> more newsletter readers -> more content data

## Key Principles
- "Value = usefulness" -- only create what genuinely helps implementation
- Pre-validate everything -- never guess what to build
- Package > content -- naming and visuals matter more than depth
- Comment-gating > direct links -- algorithmic boost from engagement
- Application model > open access -- qualifies leads, captures data
- Frequency matters -- most creators under-ask. Send 1-2 conversion emails/month minimum.
