# 4-Axis Variation Matrix

## The Core Principle

Every validated concept contains multiple distinct variations generated by changing 1 element at a time while keeping all others constant. This isolates variables so performance data tells you exactly which element is responsible for the result.

---

## Axis 1: Hook Variation (5 per concept)

| # | Category | Example Opening | Character Limit |
|---|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Specificity-Driven | "If you had a baby in the last 18 months and..." | <80 |
| 2 | Result Specificity | "I'm 22 pounds down in 10 weeks..." | <60 |
| 3 | Counterintuitive | "Stop counting calories. Seriously." | <90 |
| 4 | Before-State Immersion | "It's 6 AM and you're already exhausted." | <80 |
| 5 | Peer Observation | "I've noticed something about agency owners..." | <90 |

**Production rule:** Sections 2-6 of the script remain identical across all hook variations. Only Section 1 changes.

**Testing rule:** Post all 5 within the same week. Compare 48-hour watch-through and 7-day CTR.

---

## Axis 2: Avatar Variation (4 per concept)

| Type | Presentation | Best For | Trust Signal |
|------|-------------|----------|--------------|
| A: Aspirational | At/near end of transformation | Products with dramatic before/after | "I want to be where they are" |
| B: Relatable | Middle of journey, closer to before | Products addressing current pain | "They're just like me" |
| C: Peer Authority | Credible practitioner, knowledgeable | Professional/B2B products | "They know what they're talking about" |
| D: Demographic Variant | Different age/ethnicity/environment | Broadening audience reach | "People like me use this" |

**Production rule:** Same script, same voice-over. Different avatar only.

**Testing rule:** The avatar that converts best is almost always category-specific and must be tested.

---

## Axis 3: Length Variation (3 per concept)

| Version | Duration | Sections Included | Use Case |
|---------|----------|-------------------|----------|
| Abbreviated | 15-20s | Persona Signal + Mechanism + CTA | Retargeting, high-awareness |
| Standard | 30-35s | Signal + Abbreviated Pain + Mechanism + CTA | Default for testing |
| Full | 45-55s | All 6 sections complete | Cold audiences, first touch |

**Testing insight:** Some audiences convert better with shorter, more direct content. Some require the full narrative arc. Not predictable from first principles -- must be tested.

---

## Axis 4: Delivery Register (2 per concept)

| Register | Characteristics | Best For |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| High Energy | Enthusiasm, excitement in transformation, faster pace | Fitness, beauty, lifestyle, consumer |
| Calm Authority | Understated confidence, measured pace, no hype | Productivity, SaaS, professional, B2B |

**ElevenLabs implementation:**
- High Energy: Source recording with genuine enthusiasm. Stability 0.30-0.35.
- Calm Authority: Source recording with measured, confident tone. Stability 0.40-0.45.

---

## Combinatorial Math

```
1 validated concept
  x 5 hooks
  x 4 avatars
  x 3 lengths
  x 2 registers
= 120 possible variations
```

**In practice:** Produce 20-25 per cycle (the highest-priority combinations):

### Priority Selection for First Batch

Start with the highest-information combinations:

1. **5 hook variations** with the default avatar (B: Relatable) at standard length (30-35s) with calm authority register = 5 pieces (tests hooks in isolation)
2. **4 avatar variations** with the winning hook at standard length = 4 pieces (tests avatars in isolation)
3. **3 length variations** with the winning hook + winning avatar = 3 pieces (tests length)
4. **2 register variations** with the winning hook + winning avatar + winning length = 2 pieces (tests register)

**Total first batch:** 14 pieces that test all 4 axes with minimal combinatorial overlap. Remaining 6-11 pieces fill the best-performing combinations.

## Weekly Variation Tracking

Track every variation in the content performance spreadsheet:

| Content ID | Concept | Hook Category | Avatar Type | Length | Register | Account | CTR | Watch-Through | Revenue |
|-----------|---------|--------------|-------------|--------|----------|---------|-----|--------------|---------|
| [ID] | [name] | [1-5] | [A-D] | [15/30/45s] | [High/Calm] | [account] | [%] | [%] | [$] |

After 2 weeks of data, patterns emerge: "Counterintuitive hooks + Relatable avatars + 30s + Calm authority consistently outperform other combinations for this product/audience."

That pattern becomes the default production configuration until data shows otherwise.
