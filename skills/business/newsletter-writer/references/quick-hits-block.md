# Quick Hits / Quick Bites Block

A reusable content block for any newsletter template. Short-form links that boost CTR and create additional sponsorship inventory.

## What It Is

5-7 one-line links to stories, tools, or content that didn't make the main issue but are worth a click. Each link gets one line: emoji + bold headline + 1 sentence.

This is NOT the same as curated links (which have 2-3 sentences of commentary each). Quick hits are scannable, low-commitment, and fast.

## Why It Works

- **Scannable:** Readers who skim everything else still click quick hits
- **Low commitment:** "Just one click" feels easy compared to reading a full section
- **CTR boost:** More clickable links = higher overall click rate for the issue
- **Monetization:** Individual slots can be sold as sponsorship inventory at $5-15 CPMs
- **Content recycling:** Link to your own blog posts, social content, older issues, or YouTube videos

## Format

```
## Quick Hits

- [Emoji] **[Headline](link)** - [1 sentence, 15 words max]
- [Emoji] **[Headline](link)** - [1 sentence, 15 words max]
- [Emoji] **[Headline](link)** - [1 sentence, 15 words max]
- [Emoji] **[Headline](link)** - [1 sentence, 15 words max]
- [Emoji] **[Headline](link)** - [1 sentence, 15 words max]
```

5-7 items. Mix of:
- External links to interesting stories (2-3)
- Your own content: social posts, blog, older issues (1-2)
- Sponsor links if sold (1-2)

## Placement

- End of the issue, before sign-off
- After all main content and the sponsor block
- Before the poll (if using one)
- Works in: Roundup (default), Trends Report (optional), Deep Dive (optional), Curation (optional)

## Content Sources

Where to find quick hit items without extra research:

- Stories you researched for the main issue but didn't feature
- Your own social posts from the past week
- Older newsletter issues that are newly relevant
- Tools or resources readers asked about via replies
- Industry announcements worth knowing but not worth analyzing
- Partner content (sold sponsorship slots)

## Monetization

- Sell individual quick hit slots to sponsors at $5-15 CPMs
- Lower CPM than the main sponsor block, but higher volume (you can sell multiple)
- No special label needed (they feel editorial and native)
- Disclose if required by regulations, but format should feel like a natural recommendation

## Quality Rules

- Every link must be genuinely useful to the reader
- No dead links (check before publishing)
- Vary the emoji per item for visual scanning
- Don't pad with filler to hit 7 items. 5 strong beats 7 weak.
- Headlines should create enough curiosity to click, but not mislead
- Keep the one-sentence description factual and specific
