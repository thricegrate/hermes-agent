## Calendar Management Notes

**How to use this calendar:**
1. Fill in Month 1 completely before you start writing
2. Fill in Month 2 after publishing your first 2 issues (you'll have reader feedback by then)
3. Fill in Month 3 after publishing 6 issues (you'll know what resonates)
4. Swap topics freely: the calendar is a guide, not a contract
5. If a trending topic demands coverage, bump a planned issue to the next slot

**Tracking performance:**
After each issue, note:
- Open rate
- Click rate
- Reply count
- Which section got the most clicks
- Any reader feedback

Use this data to inform future topic and template choices.
