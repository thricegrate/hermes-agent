## Writing Notes

**Target length:** 800-1,500 words for the full issue (Section 2 should be 400-800 words)
**Tone:** Conversational but authoritative. Write like you're explaining something to a smart friend.
**Formatting tips:**
- Use bold for key terms and takeaways
- Keep paragraphs to 2-3 sentences max
- Use bullet points for lists of 3+ items
- Add a line break between sections for visual breathing room
- Use subheadings (###) to make the deep dive scannable
