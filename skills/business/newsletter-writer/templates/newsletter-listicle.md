## Writing Notes

**Target length:** 600-1,200 words for the full issue
**Tone:** Practical and direct. You're a coach giving clear instructions, not a professor lecturing.
**Number of items:** 3-7 is the sweet spot. Fewer than 3 feels thin. More than 7 loses focus.
**The headline formula:** "[Number] [Things] To [Get Outcome] With [Benefit] Even If [Obstacle]"
- Example: "5 Prompts To Write Better Emails With AI Even If You're Not a Writer"
- Example: "7 Steps To Automate Your Content Calendar With Zero Coding Experience"

**Formatting tips:**
- Number each item clearly (### #1, ### #2, etc.)
- Keep each item to a consistent length (100-200 words each)
- Use bold for the key insight or action within each item
- End each item with a clear takeaway sentence
- The "what to do next" section is critical: don't skip it
- Each list item should stand on its own (readers may only read 2-3)
