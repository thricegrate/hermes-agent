# Course Launch Sequence Blueprints

Source: LearnWorlds "Email Templates & Sequences to Boost Your Sales"

Three complete day-by-day launch sequence blueprints with exact timing, plus 4 goal-based lifecycle sequences. Each blueprint includes the cart-close triple-send pattern.

---

## 1. Seven-Day Launch Sequence (10 Emails)

Fast launch for established audiences with existing trust.

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | Course teaser video | Build anticipation, show what's coming |
| 2 | Course description/outline | Detail what they'll learn, module breakdown |
| 3 | Course goes live + testimonials + urgency | "Enrollment closes [date]" — social proof + deadline |
| 4 | Gather feedback ("Tell me something [name]") | Engagement + objection surfacing |
| 5 | FAQ + money-back guarantee | Handle objections, reduce risk |
| 6 | Freebie/bonus offer (3 gifts: checklist, 1:1 session, referral discount) | Incentivize fence-sitters |
| 7 | Thank you + testimonials | Relationship maintenance |
| 7 (9am) | Cart closing #1 — "best time to buy" argument | Logic-based urgency |
| 7 (10-11am) | Cart closing #2 — mid-day reminder | Frequency pressure |
| 7 (10pm) | Cart closing #3 — "2 hours left" | Final scarcity push |

**Key pattern:** 3 cart-close emails on the final day at different times (morning, midday, evening).

## 2. Fifteen-Day Launch Sequence (12 Emails)

Moderate timeline for audiences that need more warming.

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | Making the Announcement (countdown GIF) | Teaser with visual urgency |
| 3 | "What is this all about?" — school intro + poll | Engagement + segmentation data |
| 5 | "Did you know?" newsletter — share poll results + blog content | Social proof from poll, content value |
| 7 | Course is Live | Official launch |
| 7+ | Newsletter — community benefits + free ebook | Value-add, community selling |
| 9 | "Have you tried this?" FAQ | Objection handling |
| 11 | Thanking People + Review email (social proof) | Testimonials + gratitude |
| 13 | Bonus 1+1 Offer (mini-course or 10% discount) | Incentive for fence-sitters |
| 13+ | Newsletter — webinar invitation | Additional touchpoint |
| 15 (9am) | "Don't Forget" — Cart closing #1 (FOMO) | Morning urgency |
| 15 (12pm) | "Complete Your Order" — Cart closing #2 | Midday reminder |
| 15 (1hr before) | "Still Here? Your Cart is Waiting" — Cart closing #3 | Final push |

**Key pattern:** Poll results create social proof. Webinar adds touchpoint. Triple cart-close on Day 15.

## 3. Thirty-Day Launch Sequence (15 Emails)

Extended timeline for cold or new audiences. Builds trust before selling.

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | Course teaser video | Curiosity + anticipation |
| 6 | Course details + value proposition | What they'll learn, why it matters |
| 12 | Appearing on Media (testimonials + podcast invite) | Authority building |
| 12+ | Newsletter 1 (mini-course + FOMO statistics) | Value + urgency data |
| 15 | Follow-up (podcast recap + social proof) | Reinforce authority |
| 17 | Course is Available for the Public | Official launch (Day 17, not Day 1) |
| 19 | Newsletter 2 (checklist + testimonials) | Practical value + proof |
| 21 | FAQ Webinar | Interactive objection handling |
| 25 | Give Thanks + Gift (complimentary ebook) | Relationship building |
| 26 | Newsletter 3 (webinar CTA + blog link) | Content value + event invite |
| 28 | Newsletter 4 (infographic + research + social links) | Authority content |
| 28+ | Reward (free ebook) | Gratitude + value |
| 29 | Pre-closing cart email | Warning shot |
| 30 (9pm) | "Your Head's Up" — Cart closing #1 | Evening urgency |
| 30 (11pm) | "Are you here?" — Cart closing #2 | Late night push |
| 30 (3hr before) | "Still interested?" — Last Chance Cart closing #3 | Final scarcity |

**Key pattern:** 16 days of trust-building before launch. Multiple newsletters interspersed. Triple cart-close on Day 30.

## 4. Cart-Close Timing Pattern (Universal)

Consistent across all three blueprints:

| Send | Timing | Tone |
|------|--------|------|
| Cart Close #1 | Morning (9-10am) | Logical — "best time to buy," benefits recap |
| Cart Close #2 | Midday (11am-12pm) | Reminder — "complete your order," quick recap |
| Cart Close #3 | Evening (1-3 hours before deadline) | Scarcity — "last chance," specific spots remaining |

**Rules:**
- Never skip the triple-send on close day. It's the highest-converting pattern.
- Each email should have a different angle (logic, reminder, scarcity)
- Include testimonials in at least one cart-close email
- P.S. sections should contain a different proof point than the body

## 5. Goal-Based Lifecycle Sequences

### Onboarding/Welcoming Sequence (13 Days, 6 Steps)

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | Welcome — links to profile, community, blog | Orient new user |
| 3 | List of available courses (cross-sell) | Discovery |
| 5 | Breakdown of most popular course | Conversion nudge |
| 7 | How to navigate the course (feature list) | Reduce friction |
| 9 | How to use course materials (topic guide) | Maximize usage |
| 11 | "4 tips to succeed" with online learning | Engagement |
| 13 | What results/certification to expect | Goal-setting |

### Conversion Sequence (10 Days, 5 Steps)

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | Welcome + browse courses | Discovery |
| 3 | Introduce yourself and team | Trust building |
| 5 | Present product + video | Show value |
| 7 | Describe course elements as value | Feature-to-benefit |
| 10 | Describe course outcome (dream result) | Future pacing |

### Engagement Sequence (15 Days, 6 Steps)

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 2 | Start your first course | Activation |
| 4 | Watch your first video | Progress nudge |
| 6 | Attend your first live class | Community |
| 8 | Take your first quiz | Interaction |
| 10 | Read your first ebook | Content depth |
| 15 | Get your certification | Completion incentive |

### Upsell Sequence (10 Days, 5 Steps)

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | "Since you took this class, you might enjoy these" | Personalized recs |
| 3 | "If you liked X, you will love Y" | Similar product |
| 5 | "People who followed X also bought Y" | Social proof upsell |
| 7 | Course bundle offer at discounted price | Bundle incentive |
| 10 | Membership package pitch | Ascension offer |
