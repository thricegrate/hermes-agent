# Product Launch Sequence Template (10 Emails)

**Trigger:** Launch day (day after Pre-Launch Day 10, or standalone)
**Key rule:** Early emails educate and excite. Late emails create FOMO and deadlines.

## Sequence Setup Notes for Beehiiv

1. **Trigger:** Manual start on launch day, or auto-trigger from Pre-Launch completion tag
2. **Delays:** 24 hours between emails (daily cadence during launch)
3. **Tags:** Tag purchasers immediately and remove them from the launch sequence (don't keep selling to buyers)
4. **Opt-out:** Include in every email: "Not interested in [Product Name]? [Click here] to stop these emails. You'll keep getting the regular newsletter."
5. **Deadlines:** If you set a deadline, honor it. Remove bonuses, raise prices, or close enrollment as promised.
6. **Segment for post-launch:** Tag non-buyers as `launch-[product]-non-buyer` for potential abandoned cart or future re-launch targeting
