**Preview:** Here's what you can expect

---

[Reply to this email or fill out this quick survey, link to Beehiiv survey]

[Name]

## Sequence Setup Notes for Beehiiv

1. **Trigger:** New subscriber (any signup method)
2. **Delays:** Set delays between emails as specified (Day 0, Day 2, Day 3, Day 5, Day 7, Day 9, Day 11)
3. **Tags:** Tag subscribers who complete the welcome sequence (e.g., `welcome-complete`)
4. **Exclusions:** Exclude subscribers who purchase before Email 7 (no need to soft-pitch someone who already bought)
5. **Segmentation:** If Email 1 includes a survey, use responses to branch future emails or tag subscribers for targeted content
