## Sequence Setup Notes for Beehiiv

1. **Trigger:** Purchase confirmation tag (`purchased-[product-name]`)
2. **Delays:** As specified (immediate, Day 2, Day 4, Day 7, Day 14, Day 21)
3. **Tags:**
   - `onboarding-[product]-active` when sequence starts
   - `onboarding-[product]-complete` when sequence ends
   - `survey-responded` if they complete the Day 14 survey
   - `upsell-clicked` if they click the Day 21 CTA
4. **Exclusions:**
   - Don't send Email 6 (Upsell) if subscriber already purchased [Next Product Name]
   - If subscriber requests a refund, remove them from the sequence immediately
5. **Personalization:**
   - If survey responses (Email 5) indicate they're struggling, consider a manual follow-up
   - If they reply to any email saying they're stuck, flag for personal response
6. **Metrics to track:**
   - Product access rate (are they actually logging in / downloading?)
   - Survey response rate (aim for 20-30%)
   - Upsell conversion rate (aim for 5-10% of customers)
   - Refund rate (should decrease with good onboarding: aim for under 5%)
