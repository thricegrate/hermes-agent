# Pre-Launch Sequence Template (10 Emails)

10 emails sent over 10 days before a product launch. Each email maps to one of 10 proven content angles. Replace all [bracketed placeholders] with your specifics.

**Trigger:** Manual start, 10 days before launch
**Key rule:** 80% value, 20% anticipation. Every email ends with a teaser.

## Sequence Setup Notes for Beehiiv

1. **Trigger:** Manual start or tag-based (add tag `pre-launch-[product-name]` to start)
2. **Delays:** 24 hours between each email (daily cadence)
3. **Tags:** Tag subscribers who complete pre-launch with `pre-launch-complete`: these feed into the Launch Sequence
4. **Segmentation:** Track opens and clicks on each email. High-engagement subscribers get priority in launch sequence.
5. **Opt-out:** Include a line in later emails: "Not interested in hearing about [Product Name]? [Click here] to opt out of these emails (you'll still get the regular newsletter)."
