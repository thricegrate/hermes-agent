# Adrian's DM Optimization Data

Source: Adrian Solar's IG acquisition playbook. Data from 12,847+ DMs across 25 accounts.

## Conversation Flow Patterns

Not all conversation flows convert equally. The sequence of messages matters more than any individual message.

### High-Converting Flows

**Flow A: Question > Pain Ack > Case Study > Soft Pitch > Book (BEST)**
- Conversion rate: 34%
- Avg messages to booking: 8
- Why it works: builds trust before pitching. The case study does the selling.

Example sequence:
1. YOU: "yo how are you handling instagram DMs rn? manually or got someone on it?"
2. THEM: "yeah manually, takes forever"
3. YOU: "bet that's killing your response time lol. what's your avg response time? few hours?"
4. THEM: "yeah like 3-4 hours usually"
5. YOU: "yeah that's the problem. leads ghost if you don't reply within 30 mins. we automated this for 200+ brands. AI responds under 90 seconds 24/7. booking 15-25 calls/month avg."
6. THEM: "how does it work?"
7. YOU: "connects to your IG, handles all DM convos, qualifies leads, books calls to your calendar. sounds human. takes 10 mins to set up. you free for a 15 min call tmrw?"
8. THEM: "yeah 2pm works"

**Flow B: Compliment > Curiosity Question > Value Share > Direct Ask**
- Conversion rate: 29%
- Avg messages to booking: 6
- Why it works: shorter, more direct. Good for high-intent prospects.

**Flow C: Pattern Interrupt > Pain Agitation > Solution Tease > CTA**
- Conversion rate: 26%
- Avg messages to booking: 7
- Why it works: the pattern interrupt (unexpected opening) gets attention in crowded inboxes.

### Low-Converting Flows (AVOID)

**Flow X: Pitch > Objection Handle > Pitch Again > Give Up**
- Conversion rate: 4%
- Avg messages to booking: 12 (wastes time)
- Why it fails: feels like being sold. No trust built. Every message is a push.

**Flow Y: Small Talk > More Small Talk > Eventually Pitch > Ghost**
- Conversion rate: 3%
- Avg messages to booking: 15 (massive time waste)
- Why it fails: no urgency, no direction. Prospect gets bored and leaves.

## Qualification Signals

### Strong Buy Signals (prioritize these leads)

| Signal | Booking Rate | Action |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Asks about pricing within first 3 messages | 67% | Send directly to booking link |
| Mentions current solution isn't working | 61% | Acknowledge pain, share case study |
| Asks "how does it work exactly?" | 58% | Detailed but concise explanation, then book |
| Responds within 5 minutes consistently | 54% | High intent, match their speed |

### Weak Buy Signals (nurture, don't push)

| Signal | Booking Rate | Action |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Asks "is this automated?" | 23% | Address concern, show human-sounding examples |
| Responds after 24+ hours | 12% | Move to nurture sequence, don't rush |
| One-word replies | 8% | Ask an open-ended question to re-engage |
| Asks for "more info" | 7% | They're not ready. Send a case study and wait. |

## Profile Picture Correlation

What someone's profile picture looks like predicts how likely they are to reply.

| Profile Picture Type | Reply Rate | Why |
|---------------------|-----------|-----|
| Personal photo (face) | 34% | People trust faces. Connection before business. |
| Logo only | 19% | Feels corporate. Harder to build rapport. |
| Product photo | 16% | Transactional vibe. They expect to be sold to. |
| No profile picture | 8% | Usually inactive or spam accounts. |

**Tactical insight:** When running outreach from your own accounts, always use a personal photo. When targeting, prioritize prospects with personal photos (they reply 2x more than logos).

## Ghost Recovery Sequences

When a prospect goes dark mid-conversation, don't send "just checking in." Lead with new value.

### For leads who asked about features:
"yo just realized I didn't send you that demo video showing how [specific feature] handles [their concern]. want me to send it real quick?"

### For leads who asked about pricing:
"btw we just added a new [pricing option] that might fit your budget better. [lower price] instead of [original]. interested?"

### For leads who showed general interest:
"yo I actually just got a case study from a brand in your exact niche doing [impressive result] with our system. would probably be super relevant for you. want me to send it?"

### Timing:
- Day 3 after last contact: new value piece (video, case study, feature)
- Day 7: different angle entirely (new pricing, niche-specific proof)
- Day 14: final soft touch ("no worries if timing's off, just wanted to share this before I forget")
- After Day 14: move to long-term nurture list, no more active outreach

| Template | Week 1 | Week 2 | Week 3 | Week 4 | Status |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Process question | 34% | 31% | 26% | 19% | BURNED (retire) |

| Pattern interrupt | - | 32% | 30% | 28% | Fresh (monitor) |
| Niche-specific | - | - | 29% | 31% | New (promising) |

**Rotation rules:**
1. Drop 15%+ from peak = retire immediately
2. Declining 3 weeks in a row = retire even if small drops
3. Always have 2+ templates in "fresh" or "stable" status
4. Source new templates from: prospect replies, email subject lines that work, competitor DMs you receive
