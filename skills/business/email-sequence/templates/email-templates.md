# Email Sequence Templates

## Educational Email Course (EEC) - 5 Days

### Day 1: The Hook

**Subject:** [Outcome-focused, curiosity-driven]

Hey [First Name],

[1-2 sentences: welcome, what this email course is about]

[1 paragraph: the problem you're going to help them solve over the next 5 days]

[1 paragraph: your credibility - why you're the right person to teach this (brief)]

Here's what you'll learn this week:
- Day 1: [Today's topic] (you're here)
- Day 2: [Topic]
- Day 3: [Topic]
- Day 4: [Topic]
- Day 5: [Topic + what's next]

[2-3 paragraphs: Day 1's actual lesson content]

**Your action item:** [One specific thing to do today]

Tomorrow, I'll show you [preview of Day 2].

[Sign-off]

---

### Day 2: The Foundation

**Subject:** [Core concept name]

[Hook: 1-2 sentences connecting to Day 1]

[Main lesson: the foundational concept/framework they need (3-4 paragraphs)]

[One actionable step they can do today]

Tomorrow: [Preview of Day 3]

[Sign-off]

---

### Day 3: The How-To

**Subject:** [Step-by-step promise]

[Hook: connect to what they learned so far]

[Step-by-step methodology with a concrete example (4-5 paragraphs)]

[One actionable step they can do today]

Tomorrow: [Preview of Day 4]

[Sign-off]

---

### Day 4: The Mistake

**Subject:** [The myth/mistake they're making]

[Hook: "Most people in [niche] make this mistake..."]

[Explain the common mistake or myth (2-3 paragraphs)]

[Show what to do INSTEAD with a before/after example (2-3 paragraphs)]

[One actionable step they can do today]

Tomorrow is the final day - I'll share [preview + hint at the product].

[Sign-off]

---

### Day 5: The Bridge

**Subject:** [What's next for them]

[Hook: celebrate what they've accomplished this week]

[Recap the 4 key things they learned (quick bullet list)]

[Show the gap: "You've made great progress, but there's more to the full transformation..." (2 paragraphs)]

[Natural pitch to the product - not a hard sell, but a clear bridge:]

"If you want to go deeper and [full transformation promise], I built [Product Name] to take you there step by step."

Here's what's inside:
- [Module 1 benefit]
- [Module 2 benefit]
- [Module 3 benefit]

Plus [X] bonuses worth $[value] included free.

[CTA button/link to product landing page]

[Sign-off]
