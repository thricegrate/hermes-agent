## Step 1.5: Direction Deficit Screen (if Bucket 2)

**If you landed in Bucket 2 (No Energy), answer these before moving on:**

How many active projects/goals right now? ___

**If 3+ projects AND less than 50% effort on #1:**

This is a **Direction Deficit**, not an energy problem. Your body is downregulating because it's burning fuel with no return. See `references/performance-equation.md`.

**The Sledgehammer Room:** You're hitting every wall once. Pick one spot. Keep hitting.

## Step 2: Fear Diagnosis (Bucket 3 only)

**Name the fear specifically:**
"I'm afraid that _______________"

**Identity Protection Check (Williamson):**
- Are you avoiding this to protect your image?
- Are you "inoculating yourself from failure publicly by assuring your failure privately"?
- Are you choosing "I could have done it if I'd tried" over actually trying?

**Reality check:**
- "What's the worst that actually happens if you try and fail?"
- "What do people think about the tasks you DIDN'T go for?" (Answer: nothing. They don't think about it at all.)
- "Would you respect someone who tried and failed, or someone who never tried?"

**The Antidote: Surrender**
- [ ] I'm willing to look foolish
- [ ] I'm willing to be a beginner
- [ ] I'm willing to do this badly
- [ ] I'm willing to be seen trying
