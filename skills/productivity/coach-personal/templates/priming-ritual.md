# Priming Ritual Setup + 30-Day Tracker

Step-by-step guide to building a daily Priming practice. See `references/robbins-state-management.md` for full framework.

## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Choose Your Version

| Version | Duration | Best For |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Minimum Viable | 5 min | "I have no time" -- start here |
| Standard Priming | 10-15 min | Daily practice once the habit is set |
| 30 Minutes to Thrive | 30 min | Priming + 15 min exercise |
| Hour of Power | 60 min | Priming + exercise + incantations |

**Start with the version you'll ACTUALLY do, not the one that sounds impressive.**

### Step 2: Set the Conditions

**When:** _______________ (specific time, ideally before anything else)
**Where:** _______________ (specific place, no distractions)
**Trigger:** _______________ (what happens right before -- alarm, coffee brewing, feet hit the floor)

### Step 3: The Standard Sequence

**1. Breath of Fire (3 min)**
- 3 sets of 30 sharp, forceful nasal breaths
- Arms pump overhead with each breath
- 15-second pause between sets
- This is NOT calm breathing. The physical intensity is the point.

**2. Gratitude Visualization (3 min)**
Three specific moments you're grateful for. One at a time. Don't list -- RE-ENTER each experience.

My 3 gratitude anchors (update as needed):

1. _______________
2. _______________
3. _______________ (include at least one small/mundane thing)

**3. Healing/Loving-Kindness (3 min)**
- Visualize light entering your body, healing any pain or tension
- Expand the light outward: family, friends, colleagues
- Finally: send it to adversaries or difficult people
- Conscious choice to radiate goodwill

**4. Three to Thrive (3 min)**
Three outcomes you want to achieve, visualized as ALREADY DONE. Not hoping -- experiencing from the inside.

2. _______________
3. _______________

### Step 4: Customize Your Incantation (Optional, for Extended Versions)

Write a personal incantation. Spoken ALOUD with full physical intensity during movement.

Rules:
- Command form ("I AM" or "I NOW COMMAND" -- not "I hope" or "I want")
- Include emotional intensity words
- Design it to be spoken while walking/moving

## Minimum Viable Version (5 Minutes)

For days when time is short. The non-negotiable: do SOMETHING.

1. 1 set of 30 breaths (1 min)
2. 1 gratitude moment, fully felt (2 min)
3. 1 outcome visualized as done (2 min)
