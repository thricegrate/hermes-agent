# Wheel of Life Assessment

Use quarterly, at intake, or when the client says "I feel off but can't explain why."

---

## Instructions

Rate each domain 1-10 for current satisfaction (1 = miserable, 10 = thriving). Then rate your ideal. The gap between current and ideal reveals where energy needs to go.
