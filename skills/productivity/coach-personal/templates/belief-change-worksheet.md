# Belief Change Worksheet

Combines Dickens Process (emotional demolition) with NAC (behavioral rewiring). Use when CBT reframing hasn't broken through -- the client intellectually agrees the belief is wrong but still can't let go.

See `references/robbins-belief-change.md` for full framework details.
