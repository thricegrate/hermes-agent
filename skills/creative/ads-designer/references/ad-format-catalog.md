# Ad Format Catalog

Quick-reference for all 40 static ad templates. Use this to pick which formats to generate.

**How to use:** Pick formats by category or funnel stage, then run batch mode with your selection.

## By Funnel Stage

### TOFU (Awareness) -- Stop the scroll
Best for cold audiences who don't know your brand.

**Top picks:** #9 (Bait & Switch), #20 (Advertorial), #21 (Bold Statement), #29 (UGC Viral Post), #34 (iPhone Notes), #39 (Curiosity Gap), #40 (Post-It Ugly)

### MOFU (Consideration) -- Build the case
Best for warm audiences who've seen you but haven't bought.

**Top picks:** #7 (Us vs Them), #11 (Pull-Quote Review), #13 (Stat Surround), #19 (Highlighted Testimonial), #27 (Benefit Checklist), #31 (Comparison Grid)

### BOFU (Conversion) -- Close the sale
Best for hot audiences ready to buy.

**Top picks:** #2 (Offer/Promotion), #14 (Bundle Showcase), #37 (Hero + Promo Burst), #38 (UGC + Review Split)
