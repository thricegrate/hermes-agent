# Landing Page Analysis Prompt

## Full Analysis Prompt

Use with the `image` tool on the full-page screenshot, supplemented by page snapshot text content:

```
You are an expert CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) specialist and funnel strategist. Analyze this landing page screenshot in detail.

Context: This page is the destination of a paid ad. Analyze it from the perspective of a visitor who just clicked an ad and landed here.

1. **Above the Fold:** What does the visitor see immediately without scrolling? Headline, subheadline, hero image/video, primary CTA. Is it clear what this page is about within 3 seconds?

2. **The Offer:** What exactly is being offered? Categorize it:
   - Type: free resource / quiz / webinar / demo / trial / course / product / consultation
   - Value proposition: What does the visitor get? What problem does it solve?
   - Price/friction: Free? Email-gated? Paid? How much commitment is required?

3. **Social Proof:** What credibility elements are present?
   - Testimonials (with names/photos/titles?)
   - Company logos ("as seen in" / client logos)
   - Numbers (users, revenue, results)
   - Case studies or specific results
   - Trust badges (security, guarantees, certifications)

4. **Copy Strategy:** Analyze the written content:
   - Tone (professional, casual, urgent, aspirational)
   - Pain points addressed vs benefits highlighted
   - Specificity level (vague promises vs concrete outcomes)
   - Reading level and clarity

5. **CTA Strategy:** How is conversion driven?
   - Number of CTAs and their text
   - Primary vs secondary CTAs
   - Form fields required (email only? name + email + company?)
   - Urgency elements (countdown, limited spots, scarcity)
   - CTA button color/contrast and placement

6. **Visual Design:**
   - Layout style (long-form, minimal, multi-section)
   - Color palette and brand consistency
   - Use of imagery (real photos, stock, illustrations, screenshots)
   - Whitespace and readability
   - Mobile optimization signals

7. **Funnel Position:** Where does this page sit?
   - Top of funnel: lead magnet, quiz, free resource
   - Middle: webinar, demo, trial, consultation
   - Bottom: purchase, enrollment, subscription
   - What's the likely next step after conversion?

8. **What Works:** 2-3 specific tactical elements that are well-executed and could be replicated.

9. **Weaknesses:** 2-3 specific areas for improvement with actionable suggestions.

Be direct and tactical, this analysis is for a marketing team studying competitor funnels.
```

## Quick Analysis Prompt

For faster, lighter analysis when full detail isn't needed:

```
Analyze this landing page in 5 bullet points:
1. What's the offer? (one sentence)
2. What's the CTA? (button text + friction level)
3. What social proof is used?
4. What's the strongest element?
5. What's the weakest element?
```
